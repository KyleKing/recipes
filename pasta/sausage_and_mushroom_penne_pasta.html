Sausage And Mushroom Penne Pasta

Based on http://www.budgetbytes.com/2014/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 3-6 links Italian sausage, baked and sliced into rounds
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 8 oz container of button mushrooms
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 lb box of penne
  • Shredded parmesan, for serving

Recipe

  1. See the Oven Baked Sausage. When finished, cut into rounds
  2. In parallel, dice and prep the garlic, onions, and mushrooms
  3. Combine half of the diced tomatoes, the sausage, diced items, and the box of penne pasta. Mix in the basil, oregano, and garlic
  4. Add the rest of the diced tomatoes and 2.5-3 cups of water, then bring the pot to a rapid boil
  5. Once boiling, stir and simmer at 30% heat with a tight fitting lid. Make sure the pasta is submerged
    1. Simmer for 10 minutes, stir, then remove the lid and simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes
