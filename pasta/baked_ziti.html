Baked Ziti

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • Ziti noodles
  • 6 oz spinach
  • 1 jar (1 pound 9 ounces) pasta sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1⁄2 cup Parmesan
  • 1⁄2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1⁄4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella, divided

Recipe

  1. Prepare pasta according to the instructions
  2. Add spinach during the last minute of cook time, then drain the pasta and spinach. Place back in the pot
  3. Stir the pasta sauce, ricotta, Parmesan, garlic, black pepper, and 1⁄2 cup mozzarella
  4. Spoon pasta mixture into 9x13 inch pan and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella on top
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min

Notes

  1. If put in the fridge, extend cook time by 10-15 min!
  2. Alex’s family recipe and great for groups
