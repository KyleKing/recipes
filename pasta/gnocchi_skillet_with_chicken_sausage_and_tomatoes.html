Gnocchi Skillet with Chicken Sausage and Tomatoes

Based on https://www.thekitchn.com/recipe-gnocchi-skillet-with-chicken-sausage-amp-tomatoes-20642/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 lb frozen or shelf-stable gnocchi
  • 9 oz cooked chicken sausage (about 3 links), 1/4” coins
  • 1-2 pints of cherry or grape tomatoes, sliced in half lengthwise
  • 1-2 oz fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced (1/2-1 cup loosely packed)
  • Optionally a half bag of spinach and/or pasta sauce

Recipe

  1. Coin the sausage and half the tomatoes
  2. Start boiling water for the gnocchi
    1. Once boiling, cook the gnocchi for 2 min (follow package directions)
    2. Drain and toss with a drizzle of olive oil
  3. Heat a 10-inch or larger cast iron skillet over medium heat with a light drizzle of olive oil. Add the sausages and cook until beginning to brown (2-3 min)
  4. Push the sausages into a pile at the edge of the skillet and turn the heat up to high
    1. When the skillet is quite hot, add the tomatoes, skin down, crowding them in if necessary. Cook until they are blistered (1-2 min)
  5. Stir in the sausage and cook until both tomatoes and sausage are slightly browned, (~2 min)
    1. Stir in gnocchi and cook until all is combined, but the tomatoes have not broken down into sauce
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the basil
  7. Taste and season with salt and pepper

Notes

  1. Any cooked chicken sausage can be used, but flavored ones with red pepper or garlic work best
