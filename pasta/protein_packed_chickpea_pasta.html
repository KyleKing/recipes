Protein Packed Chickpea Pasta

Based on https://saucestache.com/the-easiest-protein-packed-pasta-meal-prep-i-make/ (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 7 oz (200g) silken tofu
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Pasta and Vegetables

  • 2 boxes chickpea pasta
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 cup peas

Recipe

  1. Combine tofu, lemon juice, parsley, dill, red onion, garlic, salt, pepper, rice vinegar, and chia seeds in a bowl. Mix well and refrigerate for at least 5-10 minutes (quick chill while pasta cooks) or 30+ minutes ahead of time to let flavors meld. Remove from fridge and stir before using.
  2. Cook chickpea pasta in salted boiling water for 8 minutes.
  3. Drain canned chickpeas and mash with a fork. Add chopped celery and shredded carrots to the bowl.
  4. Drain cooked pasta and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a hot pan, add peas, then pasta on top and mix. Fold in the chickpea mixture and chilled dressing. Combine thoroughly and serve.
