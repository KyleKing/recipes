Tomato-Butter Pasta

Based on https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Cavatappi, Rigatoni, Fettuccine
  • 2-3 tomatoes, halved horizontally (really need ripe and flavorful tomatoes)
  • 4 Tbsp butter, cold
  • 1 large garlic clove, peeled
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, plus more for serving
  • (Optional) Torn basil leaves, for serving
  • Parmesan, for serving

Recipe

  1. Start a pot to boil and cook the pasta. Make sure to reserve 1 cup pasta cooking water
  2. Meanwhile, using the large holes of a box grater:
    1. Grate the cut ends of the tomato into a large bowl
    2. Grate the butter
    3. Switch to the small holes and grate the garlic
  3. Add the red-pepper flakes and season generously with salt
  4. Return the drained pasta to the pot, along with the bowl of grated tomato and butter
  5. Set over medium-high heat and stir constantly to thicken (2-3 min). Add pasta water as needed
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with more red-pepper flakes, black pepper, basil and Parmesan as desired
