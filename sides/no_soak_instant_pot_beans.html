No-Soak Instant Pot Beans

Based on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried beans (makes ~2.5 cups
  • 4 cups water, or more to ensure the beans are submerged
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • (Optional) peeled garlic or peeled half onion

Recipe

  1. Ensure Instant Pot is not more than half full and that the beans are submerged by 2” to 3” of water
  2. Cooking Times in minutes:
    • Black beans: 20-25
    • White Beans: 25-30
    • Cannellini: 35-40
    • Chickpeas/Garbanzo: 35-40
    • Red kidney beans: 25-30, but boil for 10 min. in pot before pressure cooking to break down lectins
  3. Slow release
  4. Check that the beans are the right texture. If not, cook for 5-10 min. at high pressure
  5. Strain to use immediately or let cool and store in cooking liquid

Notes

  • One 15-ounce can of beans holds about 1.75 cups cooked beans
  • To minimize split beans, soak overnight in the fridge and cook for half the time
