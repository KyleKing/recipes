Asparagus

Based on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/howtocookasparagusonthestovetop

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1/2 - 1 lb. Asparagus
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • (Optional) garlic clove, lemon peel, etc.

Recipe

  1. Boil 3/4-1” water in a large, covered skillet. Add salt and optional clove, lemon peel, etc.
  2. Chop the inedible bottom of the Asparagus off
  3. Cover and cook the asparagus for 5-7 minutes
  4. Add additional salt, pepper, lemon juice, oil, butter, etc. before serving
