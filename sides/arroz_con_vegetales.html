Arroz Con Vegetales

Based on https://foodanddealsbylaura.com/arroz-con-vegetales/

Personal rating: Not yet rated

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 2.5 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, corn kernel)
  • 4 cups chicken broth (or water)
  • 1 tsp powder chicken bullion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp cumin (optional)
  • 1 piece jalapeño

Recipe

  1. Place rice in a container, cover with water, and let sit briefly to remove excess starch. This improves texture.
  2. Drain rice in a strainer and rinse thoroughly under running water. Shake off excess water.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in vegetable oil over medium heat. Once onion changes color, add rice and stir.
  4. Once rice changes color, add 4 cups broth (or water), cumin, salt, and chicken bullion.
  5. Cut jalapeño lengthwise and place on top of rice.
  6. Bring to high heat and allow water to boil.
  7. Cover, reduce heat to low, and cook rice 15-18 minutes.
  8. Add vegetables on top, cover again, and cook at low heat for 5 minutes.
  9. For pre-cooked vegetables: Turn off heat and cover to finish cooking with residual warmth.
  10. For fresh vegetables: Add when water boils; cook with rice.
  11. For frozen vegetables: Thaw first by soaking in water (change water twice), drain, then add after rice is cooked. Cover 5 minutes.
  12. Verify vegetables are tender and rice is fully cooked.

Notes

  • Pairs excellently with chicken, fish, or beef entrées
  • Represents authentic Mexican home-style preparation
