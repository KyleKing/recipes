Cheesy Cheeseless Scrambled Eggs With Burst Cherry Tomatoes

Based on Chrissy Teigen Cravings (wayback), pg. 16

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

Eggs

  • 12 eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp butter

Tomatoes

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes (~1 lb)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • (Optional) 1/4 cup thinly sliced chives
  • (Optional) garlic-roasted bacon

Recipe

  1. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, salt, and pepper until combined
  2. In large nonstick skillet, heat oil and butter over LOW heat until butter is melted
  3. Start heating a cast iron skillet on medium heat until hot
  4. Add eggs and cook, stirring until a custardly and forming small curds. (12-14 min). Remove from heat
  5. Make the tomatoes in parallel during the last 10 min of the egg cook time. In the hot skillet, add oil, swirl, then add the tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cook for 5-6 min until tomatoes shrivel
  6. Serve with the optional chives/bacon on top

Notes

  1. For every 4 eggs, 2.5 Tbsp cream, 1 Tbsp butter, 1 Tbsp olive oil
