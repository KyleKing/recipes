Savory Tofu Scramble

Based on Minimalist Baker (wayback), Brit’s Bites (wayback), and Walder Wellness (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Base

  • 1 (14- to 16-ounce) block firm or extra-firm tofu, drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or neutral oil
  • Salt and pepper

Mexican Sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric (for color)
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast (optional)
  • 2-3 tablespoons water or vegetable broth

Asian Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or liquid aminos
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons water

Curry Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup or honey
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2-3 tablespoons coconut milk or water

Classic Diner Sauce (with eggs)

  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives or scallions, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese

Veggie Western Sauce

  • ½ cup bell peppers (red/green mix), diced
  • ¼ cup onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: ¼ cup shredded cheese

Everything Bagel Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons vegan cream cheese (or regular cream cheese)
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened plant-based milk or water

Loaded Cheesy Herb Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (chives, parsley, or dill), chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2-3 tablespoons unsweetened plant-based milk or water
  • Optional: 2 tablespoons vegan cream cheese

Recipe

  1. Set the tofu on one of its large flat sides and cut it in half horizontally. Pat dry and wrap in towels. If time allows, press the tofu by placing a cast-iron skillet or other heavy object on top of the wrapped tofu for about 30 minutes.
  2. Choose your sauce variation and mix all sauce ingredients together in a small bowl. Set aside. Note: For Veggie Western sauce, keep vegetables separate. For Classic Diner sauce, keep eggs separate.
  3. Unwrap the tofu and season all sides with salt and pepper. Heat the oil in a medium skillet over medium-high. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown and crisp, 3 to 6 minutes per side.
  4. For Veggie Western only: Remove tofu temporarily. Add diced bell peppers and onions to the pan, cook 3-4 minutes until softened. Add garlic and tomatoes, cook 1 minute more. Return tofu to pan.
  5. Reduce heat to medium. Using a spatula or wooden spoon, break up the tofu into chunks. Add your prepared sauce and cook, stirring and continuing to break up the tofu, until the sauce is absorbed and the tofu is golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes.
  6. For Classic Diner only: Push tofu to sides of pan, add beaten eggs to center, scramble until just set, then fold together with tofu.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning.

Notes

  • Add quick-cooking vegetables (spinach, kale, bell peppers, green onions) along with the sauce. Increase seasoning if adding vegetables.

Serving Suggestions by Variation

Mexican version:

  • Black beans (see Instant Pot beans)
  • Sliced avocado
  • Pico de gallo (diced tomato, onion, jalapeño, cilantro, lime juice, salt)
  • Salsa
  • Charred corn (pan-fry kernels in dry skillet until spotted)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Warm tortillas

Asian version:

  • Quick cucumber salad (thinly slice cucumber, toss with rice vinegar, sesame oil, pinch sugar, salt)
  • Stir-fried bok choy (halve lengthwise, pan-fry cut side down, add garlic and soy sauce)
  • Kimchi
  • Steamed edamame (boil 5 min in salted water)
  • Rice
  • Sesame seeds

Curry version:

  • Basmati rice
  • Store-bought naan
  • Roasted cauliflower (toss florets with oil and curry powder, roast at 425°F for 25 min)
  • Sautéed spinach (wilt in pan with garlic)
  • Crispy chickpeas (drain, pat dry, toss with oil and spices, roast at 400°F for 30 min)

American versions (Classic Diner, Veggie Western, Everything Bagel, Loaded Cheesy Herb):

  • Hash browns (shred potato, squeeze dry, pan-fry in oil until crispy)
  • Home fries (cube potatoes, boil 5 min, pan-fry with onions until golden)
  • Roasted breakfast potatoes (cube potatoes, toss with oil, herbs, salt; roast at 425°F for 30 min)
  • Whole grain or sourdough toast
  • Toasted bagel
  • Sliced tomatoes and/or cucumbers
  • Fresh fruit or fruit salad
  • Simple arugula salad (toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt)
  • Mixed greens with lemon vinaigrette (whisk lemon juice, olive oil, Dijon, salt)
  • Hot sauce
