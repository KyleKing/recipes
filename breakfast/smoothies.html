Smoothies

Based on https://newsletter.ethanchlebowski.com/p/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 Part Frozen Base
  • 1 Part Liquid Base
  • 1/2 Part Flavor Adjuster
  • Up to 1/3 Part Supporting Flavors

Recipe

  1. Add measured ingredients to the blender in the recommended order: liquids first, then soft ingredients (yogurt, nut butter, room temp banana), then frozen items. Secure the lid and pulse to combine.
  2. Start slow, pulse, tamp, and work your way to a fast setting, then let it go for a solid 20-60 seconds

Notes

Specific Ingredients

  • Frozen Base Options
    • Frozen banana (adds creaminess)
    • Frozen mango, pineapple, or berries
    • Frozen spinach or kale (iron-rich, mild flavor)
  • Liquid Base Options
    • Almond milk, oat milk, or dairy milk
    • Coconut water
    • Orange juice (vitamin C enhances iron absorption)
  • Flavor Adjusters
    • Room temperature banana
    • Greek yogurt or kefir (protein, probiotics)
    • Nut butter (peanut, almond, cashew)
  • Dates or maple syrup (natural sweetness, iron)
  • Supporting Flavors
    • Fresh spinach or kale (iron-rich)
    • Chia seeds, hemp seeds, or pumpkin seeds (iron, omega-3)
    • Cocoa powder or cacao nibs (iron, antioxidants)
    • Fresh ginger or turmeric (anti-inflammatory)
    • Spirulina or chlorella powder (iron, protein)
    • Ground flaxseed (fiber, omega-3)

Smoothie Templates

  • Berry Blast - frozen berries + almond milk + Greek yogurt + chia seeds
  • Green Power - frozen banana + frozen spinach + coconut water + peanut butter + fresh ginger
  • Tropical Iron Boost - frozen mango + orange juice + kefir + spirulina + hemp seeds
  • Apple Cinnamon Comfort - frozen apple slices + oat milk + room temp banana + almond butter + cinnamon
  • Red Berry Flax - frozen mixed berries (frutos rojos) + almond milk + Greek yogurt + ground flaxseed (linaza) + chia seeds
  • Chocolate Protein - frozen banana + almond milk + Greek yogurt + cocoa powder + peanut butter + dates
  • Golden Turmeric - frozen pineapple + coconut water + room temp banana + turmeric + fresh ginger + black pepper
  • Pumpkin Spice - frozen banana + oat milk + Greek yogurt + pumpkin seeds + cinnamon + nutmeg + dates
  • Mango Greens - frozen mango + orange juice + fresh spinach + room temp banana + chia seeds
  • Avocado Berry - frozen berries + almond milk + avocado + room temp banana + hemp seeds

Tips

  • Smoothie shops often use frozen banana and almond milk as a base
  • I have had success with 1/2 part liquid and 1/2 part yogurt instead of all liquid
  • The 1/2 part flavor adjuster can also just be a room temperature banana if using flavorful bases
  • Don’t use ice because you’ll end up with shards, less flavor, and the smoothie will melt more quickly

Kid-Friendly Adaptations

  • Start with sweeter, milder bases (frozen banana, strawberries, mango) and avoid strong flavors like kale, ginger, or spirulina
  • Add 1-2 tablespoons of water to dilute thickness and make it easier to drink through a straw
  • Increase natural sweetness with dates, honey, or extra banana if needed
  • Try fun color combinations: “purple power” (berries + banana), “green monster” (spinach + pineapple + banana)
  • Start with small amounts of new ingredients to build acceptance (1 leaf of spinach, gradually increase)
  • Let kids help choose fruits and press the blender button for engagement

Healthy Guidance

  • Pair iron-rich ingredients with vitamin C sources (citrus, berries, kiwi, mango) to enhance absorption, but don’t pair with tea or coffee
  • Add protein (Greek yogurt, hemp seeds) and healthy fats (avocado, seeds, nut butter) for nutrients and sustained energy
  • Leafy greens provide iron, folate, and fiber without adding many calories
  • Chia and flax seeds add omega-3s and fiber for satiety - let chia soak 5-10 minutes for better texture
  • Balance fruit with protein and fat to prevent blood sugar spikes and maintain fullness
