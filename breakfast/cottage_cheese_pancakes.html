Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil, for the pan

Recipe

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low; add a small amount of butter
  3. Pour ~3 Tbsp batter per pancake; cook until bubbles form and edges look set, 2–3 minutes
  4. Flip and cook 1–2 minutes more
  5. Serve with fruit, maple syrup, or nut butter

Notes

  • Keep heat low — these brown faster than regular pancakes
  • Batter will be thinner than traditional; that’s normal
  • Kid-friendly: mild flavor, soft texture; let them pick toppings
  • Makes about 8–10 small pancakes
  • No flour needed; the oats and cottage cheese hold them together
