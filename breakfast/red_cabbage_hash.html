Red Cabbage Breakfast Hash

Based on https://www.thekitchn.com/cabbage-hash-recipe-23265895 (wayback unavailable)

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil or Butter
  • 1 medium Onion, diced
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 medium Potatoes, diced into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1/4 head Red Cabbage, thinly sliced (about 2-3 cups)
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • (Optional) 4 Eggs for topping
  • (Optional) Fresh herbs like Parsley or Chives for garnish
  • (Optional) Hot sauce for serving

Recipe

  1. Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add diced potatoes and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden and tender
  3. Push potatoes to the side and add remaining 1 Tbsp oil
  4. Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened
  5. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant
  6. Add sliced red cabbage, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together
  7. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is tender and lightly caramelized
  8. If using eggs, make 4 wells in the hash and crack an egg into each well
  9. Cover and cook for 3-5 minutes until eggs are set to your liking
  10. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with hot sauce if desired

Notes

  • Sweet potatoes work well as a substitute for regular potatoes
  • Add cooked sausage or bacon for extra protein
  • Can meal prep the hash without eggs and reheat throughout the week
  • The red cabbage will turn a beautiful purple color as it cooks
