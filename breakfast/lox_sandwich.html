Lox Sandwich

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Toasted bagel
  • Creamy & tangy cream cheese spread. Alternatively, Avocado isn’t traditional, but works
  • Cured salmon or lox*
  • Crispy & pungent onions
  • Salty & acidic capers
  • Optionally add herbs or cucumber slices

Recipe

  1. Cut a bagel in half and toast the slices to warm them through. Add layers of your desired fillings.
