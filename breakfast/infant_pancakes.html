Infant Yogurt Pancakes

Based on Mom’s on Call (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk (or skim milk + small sliver of butter)
  • 1/2 cup whole milk vanilla yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp of pureed fruit
  • Pureed fruit, for serving (optional)

Recipe

  1. Mix dry ingredients and then add the rest
  2. Make 12 1/8 cup pancakes or larger ones up 1/4 cup
  3. Flip once starting to become a darker yellow around the edges
