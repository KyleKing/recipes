Hard Boiled Eggs

Based on https://downshiftology.com/recipes/…

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Recipe

  1. Boil water in a saucepan deep enough for the eggs to sit in one layer
  2. Add eggs, cover, and reduce to a simmer. After 12 minutes, remove and place into room temp water (for variations on time, see source - egg shown in photo was put in for 6 min)

Notes

  1. Fresher eggs will take slightly longer to peel. The eggs in this recipe should be cooked straight from the refrigerator; reduce cooking times by 1 minute if using room-temperature eggs
  2. Great for salads, with stir fry and rice, ramen (add in last minute of cooking to warm the egg),
