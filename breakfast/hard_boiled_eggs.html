Hard Boiled Eggs

Based on Kenji and Ethan. Previously based on https://downshiftology.com/recipes/perfect-soft-boiled-hard-boiled-eggs

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Recipe

  1. In a deep saucepan, bring water to a rolling boil
  2. Gently lower the eggs into the boiling water
  3. Cook for ~12 minutes
  4. Transfer to an ice bath for 15 minutes
  5. Potentially chill overnight in the fridge
  6. Gently crack around the widest part, then peel under running water

Notes

  • Great for salads, sandwiches, or with stir fry and rice
