Chicken Tikka Masala

Based on https://twosleevers.com/chicken-tikka-masala/ (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup full-fat Greek yogurt (or full-fat coconut milk for dairy-free)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp liquid smoke (optional)

Sauce

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp garam masala

Finishing

  • 1/2 cup half and half (or coconut milk for dairy-free)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

For Serving

  • Basmati rice or naan bread

Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken by combining chicken pieces with yogurt, garlic, ginger, turmeric, cayenne, paprika, salt, garam masala, and liquid smoke in a bowl; mix well and set aside
  2. Add all sauce ingredients (onion, tomatoes, carrot, garlic, ginger, and all spices) to Instant Pot; stir to combine
  3. Place marinated chicken on top of sauce (do not stir in)
  4. Close Instant Pot lid and cook on high pressure for 10 minutes
  5. Allow natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then release remaining pressure manually
  6. Remove chicken from pot and set aside
  7. Use immersion blender to blend sauce until smooth and creamy
  8. Stir in half and half (or coconut milk)
  9. Return chicken to pot and stir to coat with sauce
  10. Serve over basmati rice or with naan bread; garnish with fresh cilantro

Notes

  • Serves 6
  • Prep time: 20 minutes, Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Low carb (6 net carbs per serving) and keto-compatible
  • Gluten-free
  • For 8-quart Instant Pots, double the recipe to avoid burn messages
  • Can freeze half the sauce for future meals
  • Nutrition per serving: 314 calories, 22g protein, 8g carbs
  • The yogurt marinade helps tenderize the chicken and adds authentic flavor
