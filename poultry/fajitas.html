Chicken Fajitas

Based on Little Spice Jar (wayback) and Homesick Texan (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 3 Tbsp lime juice
  • 3 Tbsp pineapple juice (no sugar added)
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tbsp avocado or olive oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp Mexican oregano
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 Tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced (optional)

Main

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 3 bell peppers (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 red or yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil for cooking

With

  • 10-12 flour tortillas
  • guacamole or sliced avocado
  • pico de gallo or salsa
  • sour cream
  • shredded cheese
  • lime wedges
  • cilantro

Recipe

  1. Whisk together all marinade ingredients. Add chicken, cover, and refrigerate 4-6 hours (or minimum 1 hour). Remove from fridge 30 minutes before cooking
  2. Heat cast iron skillet or heavy skillet over high heat. Add 1 tsp oil, then cook half the chicken 3-5 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 160°F. Rest several minutes, then slice thinly. Repeat with remaining chicken
  3. Add 1 tsp oil to skillet over high heat. Cook half the peppers and onions until sizzling and slightly charred, 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Repeat with remaining vegetables
  4. Heat tortillas by cooking each 15 seconds per side over a burner or in a dry skillet until puffed
  5. Serve sliced chicken and vegetables with warm tortillas and toppings so diners can assemble their own

Notes

  • Pineapple juice acts as a natural tenderizer—don’t marinate longer than 6 hours or chicken becomes tough
  • For more heat, substitute 4 dried chiles de arbol in the marinade or add sliced jalapeños when cooking vegetables
  • Cast iron skillet gives the best char and sizzle

Instant Pot Variation (No Marinating)

For a quick weeknight version, skip the marinade and use an Instant Pot:

  1. Slice chicken thinly and cut peppers slightly thicker. Add to Instant Pot with onions
  2. Toss with dry seasonings only: 3 Tbsp fajita seasoning blend (cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, oregano, onion powder, salt, pepper) and 1 Tbsp oil
  3. Add 1/4 cup water. Seal lid, set valve to sealing
  4. Pressure cook on high for 4 minutes, then quick release
  5. Optional: transfer chicken and vegetables to hot skillet for 2-3 minutes to add char
  6. Serve immediately with warm tortillas and toppings

Note: Pressure cooking infuses flavor without marinating. Save the cooking liquid to make fajita-flavored rice.
