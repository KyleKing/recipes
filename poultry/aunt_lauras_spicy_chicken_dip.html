Aunt Laura’S Spicy Chicken Dip

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 large can chicken
  • 1 cup Frank’s Red Hot Sauce
  • 8 oz block cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese

Recipe

  1. Heat chicken and franks until hot
  2. Stir in cream cheese and ranch
  3. Mix in 1/2 of cheese and use the rest on top
  4. Broil to heat thoroughly
