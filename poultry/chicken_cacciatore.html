Chicken Cacciatore

Based on Essential Instant Pot:69

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 6 Qt. Instant Pot
  • 2-3 lbs of boneless chicken
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced lengthwise into strips (red and yellow)
  • 1 yellow onion, rough chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • can diced tomatoes + liquid
  • 1/2 cup red wine, like Cabernet Sauvignon
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1.5 DIY Tbsp Italian seasoning (source)
    • 2 tsp dried basil
    • 2 tsp dried thyme
    • 2 tsp dried rosemary
    • 2 tsp dried marjoram
    • 2 tsp dried parsley
    • 4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • great with egg noodles or a baguette

Recipe

  1. Season chicken with salt & pepper. Place olive oil in instant pot and put thighs skin-side down. Use Sauté setting for 4 minutes.
  2. Remove chicken and add bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Sauté setting for 3 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and liquid, wine, water (broth), tomato paste, Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes, and bay leaf, combine. Add the chicken back to pot and fully coat in liquid - make sure chicken sits in one layer.
  4. In parallel, boil a pot of water and prepare the egg noodles according to package directions
  5. Seal and select Meat to cook for 12 minutes at high pressure, then quick release steam
  6. Remove the lid and choose saute for 15 minutes to reduce the sauce
