Poached Chicken

Based on https://www.ethanchlebowski.com/cooking-techniques-recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • boneless, skinless chicken (thighs or any cut)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2x 4-finger pinches of salt
  • (Optional) leftover stock, other spices, etc.

Recipe

Stove top

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. Place over medium-low heat and add salt and bay leaves, while cool taste test for sufficient salt
  3. Bring the water to 160-180 F, just below a simmer
  4. Add the chicken thighs and cook until the internal temp is 165 F (155 F if using breasts), about 15 min.
  5. Let cool and shred. Can be stored in the fridge for 5 days and used in a variety of meals, such as the dump_chicken_pot_pie

Instant Pot

  1. Pour in the broth and secure the Instant Pot lid
  2. Pressure cook for 12 minutes and quick release
