Fall/Winter Whiskey Sour

Based on Patrick’s coworker’s advice

Personal rating: 5 / 5

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 0.5 oz lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz maple syrup
  • Egg white (Optional)
  • Cinnamon stick, garnish

Recipe

  1. Combine all ingredients sans cinnamon in shaker
  2. Fill shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
  3. Strain into a whiskey glass
  4. Scrape cinnamon with side of knife over glass 5-6 times, then drop stick into glass
