Ingredients

  • 2 sprigs mint
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 0-2 tsp simple syrup
  • 2 oz Coconut cream
  • 2 oz Coconut water (or silver rum)
  • club soda (added after shaking)

Recipe

  1. Shake vigorously and top with club soda

Notes

  1. Swap Coconut water with silver rum to make alcoholic
