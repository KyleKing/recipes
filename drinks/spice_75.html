Spice 75

Ingredients

Note: serves 6

  • 2 oz syrup (60 mL)
    • 4 Tbsp sugar (60 mL)
    • 2 Tbsp water (30 mL)
    • 1 Tbsp allspice
  • 6 3/4 oz rum (200 mL)
  • 3 oz lime juice (90 mL)
  • 20 oz Champagne (600 mL)
  • Orange Spiral garnish

Recipe

  1. In a pan, warm the sugar, water, and allspice until the sugar dissolves. Let cool and strain
  2. Prep the orange spirals
  3. Pout the spiced syrup into a cocktail shaker with rum and lime juice
  4. Add ice and shake. Strain into six champagne flutes
  5. Top with champagne and add garnish
