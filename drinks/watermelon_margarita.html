Watermelon Margarita

Based on a Watermelon Mock-a-rita posted by jesskeys

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Watermelon (strained) (about 3 slices of medium watermelon blended)
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz tequila
  • 1 oz cointreau
  • pinch of sugar

Recipe

  1. Salt glasses
  2. Blend watermelon into a juice
  3. In a shaker, measure out the alcohol, lime juice, and watermelon juice
  4. Add ice and shake
  5. Serve with an ice cube and lime (or watermelon) garnish
