Coffee

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • Hot Coffee
    • Starbucks Cafe Verona
  • Iced
    • Starbucks Dark Roast Unsweetened Iced Coffee (Purple Band, Green Lid)
    • Stok Unsweetened Extra Bold Cold Brew (Vertical Purple Strip)
  • Accompaniments
    • Original Nestle Coffee Mate
    • Caramel Macchiato International Delight Coffee Creamer

Recipe

  1. Per cup of coffee:
    1. 2 scoops of ground coffee
    2. fill to the “2” line for water
    3. switch to the “mug” setting, if making a single cup
    4. Add 2 tsp coffee mate
    5. 1.5 tsp sugar
  2. To make ice coffee:
    1. Fill a small cup with ice
    2. Fill with ice coffee
    3. Add a small amount of creamer to taste
