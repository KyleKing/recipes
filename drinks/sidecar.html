The Sidecar

From the Bartender

Personal rating: Not yet rated

Ingredients

  • 2 oz (60 ml) Brandy
  • 10oz (30 ml) Cointreau
  • 1/2 oz (5 ml) lemon juice

Recipe

  • Combine in a shaker over ice
  • Shake until well mixed
  • Strain and serve in a cocktail glass
