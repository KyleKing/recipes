The Sidecar

From the Bartender

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 2 oz (60 ml) Brandy
  • 10oz (30 ml) Cointreau
  • 1/2 oz (5 ml) lemon juice

Recipe

  1. Combine in a shaker over ice
  2. Shake until well mixed
  3. Strain and serve in a cocktail glass

Notes

  1. Also see the variant from the Cocktail Codex
