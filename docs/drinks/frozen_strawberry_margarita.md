## Ingredients

* [ ] 1 lb fresh strawberries, hulled
* [ ] 1 cup silver tequila
* [ ] 2/3 cup fresh lime juice
* [ ] 1/3 cup agave, honey, or simple sugar
* [ ] 1/4 cup triple sec
* [ ] 5 cups ice

## Recipe

* Blend until smooth

!!! note
    Don't make in a subpar blender or if so, strain out the chunks
