## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 sprigs mint
- [ ] 1 oz lime juice
- [ ] 0-2 tsp simple syrup
- [ ] 2 oz Coconut cream
- [ ] 2 oz Coconut water (*or silver rum*)
- [ ] club soda (added after shaking)

## Recipe

- Shake vigorously and top with club soda

## Notes

- Swap Coconut water with silver rum to make alcoholic
