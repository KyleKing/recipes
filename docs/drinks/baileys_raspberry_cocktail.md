## Ingredients

* [ ] 2 oz Baileys
* [ ] 5 raspberries
* [ ] 1/4 cup crushed ice
* [ ] Whipped cream
* [ ] Chocolate Syrup

## Recipe

* Crush the ice into pebble size
* Place the raspberries at the bottom of the glass
* Add the crushed ice, then the baileys and optional garnish (whipped cream/chocolate syrup)
