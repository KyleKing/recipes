## Ingredients

* Seltzer
    * Grapefruit or lemon seltzer (avoid citrus & orange)
* Yogurt's
    * Chobani Cherry
    * Chobani Raspberry
    * Siggi's Raspberry
    * Siggi's Vanilla
* Dessert
    * Chocolate Butter Cream Frosting (avoid cream cheese frosting)
* Other
    * Kettle Jalapeno Chips
    * Laughing Cow Cheese Wedges - Swiss
