## Ingredients

* [ ] 1/4 cup kernels
* [ ] ~1 tbsp butter
* [ ] ~1 tsp cinnamon
* [ ] ~2 tsp sugar

## Recipe

* Place in popper and cook for 2:20 in our 900 watt microwave
* Microwave the butter until softened then mix with the popcorn. Coat with the cinnamon and sugar
