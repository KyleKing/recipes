## Ingredients

* [ ] chicken, pan-seared
* [ ] rice
* [ ] cucumber, chopped
* [ ] carrot, chopped
* [ ] 3 tsp, garlic, minced or powder
* [ ] 3 tsp soy sauce
* [ ] 2 tsp Sriracha or Sriracha mayo
* [ ] 1 tsp sugar
* [ ] 1 tsp lime juice
* [ ] cilantro, chopped
* [ ] (optional) chopped Thai chilies

## Recipe

* Cook chicken and rice, then toss with ingredients
