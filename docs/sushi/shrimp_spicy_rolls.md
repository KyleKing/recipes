## Ingredients

- [ ] cooked shrimp, chopped
- [ ] cucumber
- [ ] mayo
- [ ] Sriracha
- [ ] sushi rice
- [ ] wasabi
- [ ] nori

## Recipe

- Cut the Nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the nori, then flip rice side down
- Add the fillings and roll. Drizzle with extra spicy mayo sauce on top
