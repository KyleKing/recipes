## Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings || https://www.budgetbytes.com/2013/10/slow-cooker-chicken-dumplings/

- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 medium yellow onion
- 3 ribs celery
- ½ lb (3-4) carrots
- 1 large (¾ lb.) chicken breast
- 1 whole bay leaf
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1 tsp dried thyme
end

Dumplings:

- 1½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ Tbsp dried parsley
- 6 Tbsp cold butter
- ¾ tsp sugar
- ⅔ cup milk
end
