Roasted Shrimp Tacos

Based on http://www.howsweeteats.com/…

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb raw jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 Tbsps olive oil, divided
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Kosher salt and pepper
  • 1/2 green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro, plus more for topping
  • 2 Tbsps freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 cup corn
  • 12 4-inch flour or corn tortillas
  • Pickled onions, for topping
  • Cotija cheese, for topping

Recipe

  1. Heat oven to 400°F
  2. Combine shrimp with 1 Tbsp olive oil in a bowl. Season with smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on a sheet pan and roast for 10 minutes
  3. While shrimp roasts, prepare slaw by mixing cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, remaining 1 Tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper
  4. Add corn to the pan with shrimp. Roast another 5-6 minutes
  5. Serve on tortillas topped with slaw, pickled onions, shrimp, corn, and cotija cheese
