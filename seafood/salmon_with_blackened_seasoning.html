Salmon with Blackened Seasoning

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Salmon (or Chicken)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp Kosher Salt
  • Neutral oil or butter for blackening

Recipe

  1. Heat a Cast Iron pan on medium heat
  2. Season the fish, then sear face down for 8 min. Flip and cook for another 10-15 until done
  3. Cook salmon until internal temperature is between 130F (medium-rare) to 145F
