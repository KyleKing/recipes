One Pot Shrimp Scampi Spaghetti

Loosely adapted from https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 4 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lbs raw shrimp; defrosted, peeled, and deveined
  • 2.5 cups water and/or stock
  • 1/2 lb spaghetti
  • Frozen peas
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley (or substitute with a mix of dried parsley, basil, and oregano)
  • Lemon juice, half of a lemon
  • Parmesan cheese, 1 cup

Recipe

  1. Defrost shrimp 24 hours in fridge
  2. Peel shrimp (and de-vein if needed)
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter with olive oil. Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute
  4. Add wine, salt, red pepper flakes, and plenty of black pepper. Bring to a simmer and let the wine reduce by half (a few minutes)
  5. Add shrimp and saute until lightly pink (~3 minutes)
    1. Remove shrimp and set aside
  6. Add water and/or stock and bring to a boil
  7. Add the pasta and cook following package directions (7-8 min).
    1. If sticking, add more water as needed
    2. In the last minute, add frozen peas and bring back to a boil
  8. Stir in the parsley and lemon juice
  9. Top with the shrimp, more parsley, and Parmesan
