Pescado a Las Dos Salsas Estilo Contramar

Based on https://mesasanamx.com/2022/07/27/pescado-a-las-dos-salsas-estilo-contramar/

Personal rating: Not yet rated

Ingredients

Salsa Talla

  • 4 cascabel chiles or 1 pasilla chile, seeds removed
  • 2 árbol chiles, seeds removed
  • 1 guajillo chile, seeds removed
  • 1 pasilla chile, seeds removed
  • 1 ancho chile, seeds removed
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves
  • 5 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 3-4 saladette tomatoes
  • 1/4 white onion
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp achiote
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Parsley Sauce

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups parsley with stems
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Pinch of cumin
  • Salt

Fish

  • 1.5 to 2 kg red snapper (huachinango), butterfly cut, descaled

Recipe

  1. Toast chiles on a griddle; blend with all salsa talla ingredients. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  2. Blend all parsley sauce ingredients. Adjust seasoning and consistency with olive oil if needed.
  3. Wash and dry fish; place open on foil-lined baking sheet. Add salt.
  4. Spread sauce on each side; add more salt and pepper.
  5. Bake at 200°C for 15-20 minutes, being careful not to overcook.
  6. Serve with rice, beans, avocado, lime, and tortillas.
