Salmon with Dijon Mustard Sauce

Based on https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/…

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Salmon Filet with skin
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • 1.5 Tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • pinch of kosher salt
  • pinch cayenne pepper

Recipe

  1. Combine sauce in a small bowl and mix
  2. Preheat oven to 400
  3. Place on tin foil and fold up the sides
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until internal temperature of 120-130

Notes

  1. Recipe has mostly been cut in half to reduce the volume of the sauce and reduce the proportion of Dijon mustard
