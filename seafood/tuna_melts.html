Tuna Melts

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 2 cans of Tuna
  • 4-5 English Muffins
  • 1 pickle
  • Your Choice of Cheese slices (Muenster, Provolone, Havarti, Pepper Jack, Swiss, Cheddar, etc.)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to Broil (~10 min)
  2. Chop pickle and mix ingredients together. Toast English muffins
  3. On a foil-lined pan, cover the muffins with the tuna, then a slice of cheese. Broil until the cheese has melted

Notes

  1. Can be made in a pinch by toasting the English muffins, then topping them and steaming them in a hot pan with a lid
