Tuna Melts

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (5 oz each) Tuna, drained
  • 2-3 Tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 2-3 Tbsp chopped Dill Pickle
  • 1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • 4-5 English Muffins, split
  • 8-10 slices Cheese (Muenster, Provolone, Havarti, Pepper Jack, Swiss, Cheddar, etc.)

Optional additions for flavor/health

  • 2 Tbsp finely diced Celery (adds crunch)
  • 1 Tbsp finely diced Red Onion or 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 Tbsp Greek Yogurt (can replace half the mayo for lighter version)
  • Fresh Dill or Parsley

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to Broil (~10 min)
  2. In a bowl, combine drained tuna, mayonnaise, chopped pickle, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Add any optional ingredients if using. Mix well
  3. Toast English muffin halves until lightly golden
  4. On a foil-lined baking sheet, spread tuna mixture on each muffin half, top with cheese slice
  5. Broil for 2-3 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly

Notes

  1. Can be made in a pinch by toasting the English muffins, then topping them and steaming them in a hot pan with a lid
