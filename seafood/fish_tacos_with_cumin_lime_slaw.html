Easy Fish Tacos with Cumin Lime Slaw

Based on https://www.budgetbytes.com/fish-tacos-with-cumin-lime-slaw/ (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Cumin Lime Coleslaw

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 lime (1 Tbsp juice)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 of a 16 oz bag coleslaw mix (shredded cabbage and carrots)
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Tacos

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil (divided)
  • 1 lb cod fillets
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced

Recipe

  1. Toast tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat until browned on each side; stack and cover with foil.
  2. Prepare the coleslaw dressing by combining mayonnaise, lime juice, cumin, cayenne, salt, and sugar.
  3. Mix coleslaw base with green onions and dressing; refrigerate until needed.
  4. Season fish pieces with combined chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and salt.
  5. Cook fish in batches using oil until firm, opaque, and flaked easily (few minutes per side).
  6. Assemble tacos with fish, avocado slices, coleslaw, and jalapeño on tortillas.

Notes

  • Suitable alternatives include tilapia, mahi mahi, or halibut instead of cod
