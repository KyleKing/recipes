Philadelphia Rolls

Based on https://pickledplum.com/philadelphia-roll/ (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon (cold-smoked preferred)
  • Cream cheese, softened
  • Cucumber
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Recipe

  1. Cut nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the nori, then sprinkle with sesame seeds if using
  2. Flip the nori so rice side is down
  3. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese over the nori
  4. Add strips of cucumber and smoked salmon
  5. Roll tightly and slice into 6-8 pieces

Notes

  1. The cream cheese balances the intense smokiness of the salmon, making this a perfect fusion roll
  2. Cold-smoked salmon has better texture for sushi than hot-smoked
  3. Use thin slices of smoked salmon to prevent overwhelming the other flavors
  4. Can add avocado for extra creaminess
  5. This Western-style roll works precisely because it doesn’t try to replicate delicate fresh fish nigiri
