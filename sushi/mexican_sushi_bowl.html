Mexican Sushi Bowl

A deconstructed approach inspired by Mexico City’s fusion sushi scene

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon, sliced
  • Sushi rice
  • Black beans, cooked
  • Corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or grilled)
  • Avocado, diced or sliced
  • Pico de gallo or salsa
  • Pickled jalapeño slices
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Radishes, thinly sliced
  • Cilantro, fresh
  • Queso fresco, crumbled
  • Lime wedges

Chipotle-Avocado Dressing

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder or chipotle in adobo
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 Tbsp water to thin

Recipe

  1. Prepare sushi rice and let cool
  2. Make the chipotle-avocado dressing by blending all ingredients until smooth
  3. Arrange rice in bowls
  4. Top with black beans, corn, cucumber, radishes
  5. Add smoked salmon, avocado, and pico de gallo
  6. Garnish with pickled jalapeños, fresh cilantro, and queso fresco
  7. Drizzle with chipotle-avocado dressing
  8. Serve with lime wedges

Notes

  1. This bowl captures Mexico City’s love for mixing Japanese and Mexican flavors
  2. Black beans and corn add authentic Mexican elements
  3. Chipotle-avocado dressing replaces cream cheese-based sauces
  4. The combination is fresh, spicy, and satisfying
  5. Queso fresco adds a salty, creamy element without being heavy
  6. Great for meal prep - keep components separate and assemble when ready to eat
  7. Can use any salsa you prefer - mild to spicy based on your taste
