Shrimp Spicy Rolls

Based on http://fromgatetoplate.com/2014/04/14/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • cooked shrimp, chopped
  • cucumber
  • mayo
  • Sriracha
  • sushi rice
  • wasabi
  • nori

Recipe

  1. Cut the Nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the nori, then flip rice side down
  2. Add the fillings and roll. Drizzle with extra spicy mayo sauce on top
