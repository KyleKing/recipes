California Rolls

Based on https://makemysushi.com/Recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 6 sticks Surimi (Imitation crab)
  • 1/2 avocado
  • cucumber
  • sesame seeds
  • sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Masago (flying fish roe) or Tobiko

Recipe

  1. Cut the Nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the Nori and top with sesame seeds, then flip rice side down
  2. Add strips of the Surimi, avocado, cucumber, and choice of fish roe then roll

Notes

  1. Tobiko is fish eggs with corn syrup and food coloring
  2. See more detailed guide here (wayback)
