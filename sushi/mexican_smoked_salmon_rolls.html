Mexican Smoked Salmon Rolls

Inspired by Mexico City’s sushi fusion scene, particularly Sinaloan-style “culichi” sushi

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon (cold-smoked preferred)
  • Ripe avocado
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Pickled jalapeño slices
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Queso fresco (optional crumbled topping)
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori

Chipotle-Soy Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder or 1 tsp minced chipotle in adobo
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp honey or agave syrup (optional)

Recipe

  1. Make the chipotle-soy sauce by mixing all ingredients
  2. Prepare sushi rice and let cool
  3. Cut nori sheets in half
  4. Spread rice over nori, flip so rice side is down
  5. Add smoked salmon, avocado slices, cucumber strips, and jalapeño
  6. Roll tightly and slice into pieces
  7. Top with fresh cilantro and crumbled queso fresco (optional)
  8. Serve with chipotle-soy sauce

Notes

  1. Chipotle-soy sauce provides the smoky-spicy element characteristic of Mexican sushi
  2. Avocado replaces cream cheese as the creamy element
  3. Pickled jalapeño adds authentic Mexican heat without being overwhelming
  4. Queso fresco is lighter than cream cheese but adds a salty, creamy finish
  5. Lime is essential - squeeze fresh lime over the rolls before eating
  6. This captures Mexico City’s fusion flavors without relying on heavy cream cheese
