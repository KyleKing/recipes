Canned Spicy Tuna Roll

Based on https://makemysushi.com/Recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp mayo
  • 1 tsp Chili sauce (Sriracha)
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • spoon of masago (fish roe) (optional)
  • a bit of soy sauce (optional)
  • can tuna
  • sesame seeds
  • cucumber (cut into quarters, remove wet seeds, cut into thirds)
  • 2 green onions, cut into thin rounds for topping
  • sushi rice
  • nori

Recipe

  1. Mix the sauce, then add and mix with the canned tuna
  2. Cut the Nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the nori and top with sesame seeds, then flip rice side down
  3. Add the spicy tuna and cucumber and roll
  4. Top with a dab of spicy mayo and green onion round
