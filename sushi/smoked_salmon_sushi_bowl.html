Smoked Salmon Sushi Bowl

Based on https://themodernproper.com/salmon-sushi-bowl (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon, sliced
  • Sushi rice
  • Cucumber, diced or julienned
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Edamame, shelled
  • Carrots, julienned or shredded
  • Seaweed salad (optional)
  • Pickled ginger
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nori sheets, cut into strips
  • Soy sauce
  • Sriracha or spicy mayo (optional)
  • Rice vinegar

Seasoning

  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Recipe

  1. Prepare sushi rice according to package directions
  2. While rice is cooking, prepare the seasoning by mixing rice vinegar, sugar, and salt
  3. Once rice is cooked, fold in the seasoning mixture and let cool
  4. Arrange cooled rice in bowls
  5. Top with smoked salmon, cucumber, avocado, edamame, and carrots
  6. Add seaweed salad, pickled ginger, and nori strips
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds
  8. Serve with soy sauce and optional sriracha or spicy mayo

Notes

  1. This modern deconstructed approach is flexible and easy to customize
  2. Use whatever vegetables you have on hand - radishes, corn, or greens work well
  3. The smoked salmon provides rich, smoky flavor throughout the bowl
  4. Great for meal prep - keep components separate and assemble when ready to eat
  5. Can add a soft-boiled egg or extra protein if desired
