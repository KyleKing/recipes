Smoked Salmon Nigiri

Based on https://makemysushi.com/Recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

No image yet

Ingredients

  • smoked salmon
  • sushi rice
  • nori

Recipe

  1. Slice salmon into pieces to cover the sushi rice. Will look best with the fat lines running diagonally when wrapped
  2. Using two index fingers and your thumb shape a bite size clump of sushi rice
  3. Optionally add a bit of wasabi underneath the salmon
  4. Using your second hand’s thumb and index finger, wrap the salmon over the rice

Notes

  1. Look for smoked salmon with the most visible white fat lines
