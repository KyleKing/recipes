Smoked Salmon Mango Rolls

Based on https://www.food.com/recipe/smoked-salmon-and-mango-sushi-with-citrus-soya-dipping-sauce-84323 (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon (cold-smoked preferred)
  • Ripe mango, cut into strips
  • Cucumber, cut into strips
  • Avocado (optional)
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori

Citrus-Soya Dipping Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 tsp grated garlic

Recipe

  1. Prepare the citrus-soya dipping sauce by whisking all ingredients together
  2. Cut nori sheet in half. Spread rice over the nori
  3. Flip the nori so rice side is down
  4. Add strips of mango, cucumber, and smoked salmon (and avocado if using)
  5. Roll tightly and slice into 6-8 pieces
  6. Serve with the citrus-soya dipping sauce

Notes

  1. The sweetness of mango pairs beautifully with the smoky salmon
  2. Citrus-soya sauce brightens and balances the rich flavors
  3. Use ripe but firm mango for best texture
  4. Cold-smoked salmon has better texture than hot-smoked for this preparation
  5. The fusion of sweet, smoky, and tangy flavors makes this a modern favorite
