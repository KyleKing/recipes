Cottage Cheese Snacks

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Scrambled Eggs

Stir 2–3 Tbsp cottage cheese into beaten eggs before cooking. Makes them fluffier and more filling. Season as usual.

Dip or Spread

Blend cottage cheese with 1 garlic clove, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, and fresh herbs (parsley, oregano, chives). Use as a dip for vegetables or pita, or spread on toast.

Toast

Spread on toast with sliced tomato, a drizzle of olive oil, and salt. Add everything bagel seasoning or red pepper flakes.

Smoothies

Add 1/4 cup to a smoothie in place of or alongside Greek yogurt. Blends smooth, minimal flavor impact.

Notes

  • Blending cottage cheese first eliminates the curd texture if that’s a concern
  • Full-fat cottage cheese has a richer flavor; low-fat works fine in cooked applications
