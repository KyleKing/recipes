How to cut a Mango

Based on https://www.mango.org/how-to-cut-a-mango

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Recipe

  1. Slice vertically to the side of the seed
  2. Peel off the skin around the piece with the seed. Slice off small pieces and use a spoon to scrape off remaining fruit from the seed
  3. On the thin piece, slice vertically, then scoop out the pieces with a spoon
