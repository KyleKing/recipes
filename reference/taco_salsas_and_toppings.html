Pico de Gallo (Fresh Salsa)

Pico de Gallo Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced (seeds removed for less watery)
  • 1/2 medium white or red onion, finely diced
  • 1-2 jalapeños, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • (Optional) 1 clove garlic, minced

Pico de Gallo Recipe

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Let sit for 15-30 minutes for flavors to meld
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve fresh

Pico de Gallo Notes

  • Best used within 24 hours as tomatoes release water over time
  • For less heat, remove all jalapeño seeds and membranes

Salsa Verde (Green Tomatillo Salsa)

Salsa Verde Ingredients

  • 1 lb tomatillos, husks removed and rinsed
  • 1-2 jalapeños or serrano peppers
  • 1/2 medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice

Salsa Verde Recipe

  1. Place tomatillos and peppers in a pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Simmer until tomatillos turn olive green and are soft (8-10 min)
  2. Drain and transfer tomatillos and peppers to a blender
  3. Add onion, garlic, cilantro, salt, and lime juice. Blend until smooth or leave chunky based on preference
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve warm or cold

Salsa Verde Notes

  • For roasted version: Broil tomatillos, peppers, onion, and garlic until charred before blending
  • Stores well in refrigerator for 5-7 days or freeze for longer storage

Salsa Roja (Roasted Red Salsa)

Salsa Roja Ingredients

  • 6-8 Roma tomatoes
  • 2-3 dried guajillo or árbol chiles (or 1-2 jalapeños for fresh)
  • 1/2 medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice or vinegar

Salsa Roja Recipe

  1. Broil tomatoes, chiles (or jalapeños), onion, and garlic on a baking sheet until charred (10-15 min), turning once
  2. If using dried chiles, soak in hot water for 10 minutes to soften, then remove stems and seeds
  3. Transfer all roasted ingredients to a blender or food processor
  4. Add salt, cumin, and lime juice. Blend to desired consistency
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning

Salsa Roja Notes

  • For spicier salsa, include chile seeds
  • Can be served warm or cold
  • Stores in refrigerator for up to one week

Quick Pickled Red Onions

Pickled Onions Ingredients

  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup lime juice or vinegar (white, apple cider, or rice vinegar)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • (Optional) 1/4 tsp dried oregano or cumin

Pickled Onions Recipe

  1. Place sliced onions in a jar or bowl
  2. Add lime juice/vinegar, salt, sugar, and optional spices
  3. Shake or stir to combine
  4. Let sit for at least 15 minutes (or up to several hours for more flavor)
  5. Drain before serving or serve with some liquid

Pickled Onions Notes

  • Stores in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks
  • Add jalapeño slices for pickled jalapeños
  • Great on tacos, salads, and sandwiches

Mexican Crema (or Lime Crema)

Crema Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Mexican crema
  • 1-2 Tbsp milk or lime juice (to thin)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • (Optional) 1/2 tsp lime zest

Crema Recipe

  1. Whisk together sour cream and milk/lime juice until smooth and drizzleable
  2. Add salt and optional lime zest
  3. Taste and adjust consistency with more liquid if needed

Crema Notes

  • For dairy-free: Use cashew cream or coconut cream
  • Stores in refrigerator for up to one week

Simple Cabbage Slaw

Cabbage Slaw Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage (green, red, or mix)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrot
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil or mayo
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • (Optional) 1/4 tsp cumin or cilantro

Cabbage Slaw Recipe

  1. Combine cabbage and carrot in a bowl
  2. Whisk together lime juice, oil/mayo, salt, and optional seasonings
  3. Toss slaw with dressing
  4. Let sit for 10 minutes before serving

Cabbage Slaw Notes

  • Add jalapeño or hot sauce for spicy slaw
  • Best used within 2-3 days
