Yogurt Dill Sauce

Similar to something like https://minimalistbaker.com/zesty-dill-yogurt-sauce

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp Dried Dill
  • 2 Tbsp Lemon juice
  • 5 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • (Optional) 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • (Optional) drizzle of Olive Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix and store in the fridge for at most a few days

Notes

  • Great with fish and as a sandwich
