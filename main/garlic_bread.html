Garlic Bread

Based on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/… and https://www.washingtonpost.com/recipes/…

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • ~2-4 Garlic Cloves, grated or very finely diced to a paste
  • ~2-4 Tbsp butter
  • ~2 Tbsp Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • Bread Loaf
  • ~3 Tbsp Cheese, grated

Recipe

  1. Position rack in middle to upper third of the oven. Preheat to 400F
  2. Combine everything except for the cheese in a microwave safe bowl and microwave until liquid (1 min + ~15s intervals)
  3. Spread evenly on each half and top with cheese
  4. Bake for 10-15 min until golden and crispy
    1. Then turn up to a broil for a few minutes to finish
  5. Slice and serve

Notes

  1. Extra Tips
  2. Also consider chives or parsley
