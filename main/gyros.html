Gyros

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1-2 chicken breast (should be lamb)
  • 2 naan slices
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • fries
  • lettuce

Tzatziki Sauce

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber peeled, seeded, and finely diced
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp dill, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp mint, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Recipe

  1. Bake the chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic
  2. Lay everything out on the Naan and cover in Tzatziki

Notes

  1. From the Joy of Cooking page 567
  2. Store bought Tzatziki makes this much easier because it’s hard to get the right amount of Dill
