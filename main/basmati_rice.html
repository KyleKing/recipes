Basmati Rice

Inspired by https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/…

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • Scaled to 1 cup of dry Basmati rice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp (or 1 tsp) Thyme, Basil, Parsely, etc.
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Recipe

  1. Rinse the Basmati rice with cold water until clear
  2. Follow package directions (typically boil 2 cups water for every cup of rice)
  3. Per 1 cup of dry rice, add 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp Thyme, and/or other herbs
    1. Add rice and return to boil
  4. Lower the temperature to simmer. Cover for 30-40 minutes until soft and all water has been absorbed
  5. On medium-low, add 1 Tbsp of butter per cup of rice. Stir until fully melted and coated
  6. Add 1 tsp of lemon juice, fluff with a fork or wooden spoon, and serve

Notes

  1. An instant pot variant can be found here https://pipingpotcurry.com/instant-pot-basmati-rice/…
    1. 1.25 cup water per cup of dry rice
    2. 4 minutes on high pressure, then allow 5 minutes for natural pressure release
