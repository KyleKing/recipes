Green Pepper and Feta Pizza

Based on https://www.washingtonpost.com/graphics/… (wayback unavailable) (wayback unavailable)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • Bag of pre-kneaded dough, let rest at room temperate for 2+ hours
  • Marinara sauce (Rao’s, etc.)
  • Green Peppers, sliced and sauteed
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese or chunks from a Mozzarella ball
  • Chunks of Feta
  • (Optional) spinach, tomatoes, red pepper flakes, basil, etc.

Recipe

  1. Put the cold pizza steel in a cold oven. Heat to 500 and allow sufficient time for the Steel to heat up (30+ min)
  2. Prep a lightly floured surface and roll out the dough until very thin using a rolling pin
  3. Put a thin layer of flour on the pizza peel and transfer the dough
    1. Spread a (very) thin layer of sauce (2-3 spoons) and quickly add the toppings
    2. To prevent sticking, intermittently shake the peel to make sure the pizza slides around easily
  4. With a jittery back and forth motion, slide the pizza onto the steel
  5. Bake for 10-25 min until the crust has browned. Use the peel to transfer to a large cutting board to slice
  6. Wash the pizza steel/stone without soap
