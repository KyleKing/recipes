Lentil Tinga-Style Filling

Adapted from lentils.md research document

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Optional: 1 Tbsp olive oil for sauteing
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced (or 1 can diced tomatoes, 14 oz)
  • 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo, minced
  • 1 Tbsp adobo sauce (from chipotle can)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

  1. (Optional) Saute onion and garlic in olive oil in Instant Pot on saute mode for 2-3 minutes
  2. Add lentils, tomatoes, chipotle peppers, adobo sauce, oregano, and broth
  3. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes
  4. Natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure
  5. Season with salt to taste. If mixture is too liquid, use saute mode to reduce for a few minutes

Notes

  • Versatile filling for tacos, tostadas, burrito bowls, stuffed peppers, or quesadillas
  • Adjust chipotle amount based on heat preference (start with 1 pepper for mild)
  • Much lower saturated fat than traditional chicken or pork tinga
  • Stores well in refrigerator for 4-5 days; freezes well
  • Brown lentils (similar to green) hold their shape well for this filling and provide a peppery flavor that complements the smoky chipotle
  • For toddlers: Reserve portion before adding chipotle, or serve with cooling toppings like avocado and sour cream
