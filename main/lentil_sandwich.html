Lentil Sandwich

Based on https://saucestache.com/why-you-should-be-eating-lentil-sandwich (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • White Onion, diced
  • Green Pepper, diced
  • 1-2 Carrots, diced
  • 2-3 Garlic Cloves, diced
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup Green or Red lentils (or rice)
  • 1.5 cups Vegetable Broth
  • 1/2 cup Sauce (Curry, Steak, etc.)
  • Bread

Recipe

  1. Prep the vegetables and garlic, then saute in a deep pan for a few minutes
  2. Add lentils, broth, and sauce. Mix and let simmer for 20 min

Notes

  • If using a 2 cup can of broth, you can scale up the ingredients, as long as they fit in the pan, by adding another 1/4 cup of rice or lentils and extra vegetables. Or you can cook the extras separately if using a main sauce that is too flavorful for kids
  • Green lentils hold shape better for sandwiches and have a peppery flavor; red lentils cook faster (15 min vs 20 min), break down into a creamy spreadable texture, and have a nutty flavor
  • Optional: Add a squeeze of lemon or lime before serving
