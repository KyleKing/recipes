Cottage Cheese Savory Bowl

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 2 Tbsp red onion, diced
  • 2 Tbsp feta, crumbled
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or chives, chopped

Recipe

  1. Spoon cottage cheese into a bowl as the base
  2. Arrange tomatoes, cucumber, and onion on top
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice
  4. Top with feta, herbs, and season with salt and pepper

Notes

  • Swap feta for avocado or add both
  • A pinch of red pepper flakes or dried oregano fits well
  • Serve with pita or crackers to make it more filling
  • Black bean salad or chickpea salad toppings work as a shortcut
