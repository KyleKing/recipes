Black Bean Salad

Loosely adapted from this Reddit post (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • Can of black beans
  • Can of corn
  • Medium red onion, diced
  • Avocado, diced
  • Large bell pepper, diced
  • Jalapeno, diced
  • Tomato, diced or cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • Feta, crumbled

Recipe

  1. Combine Ingredients in a large bowl, toss, and rest in the fridge for an hour

Notes

  1. Can also use leftover Taco seasoning or add Mexican Oregano or dried marjoram
