Hummus

Based on this recipe from the Washington Post (wayback). Another alternative is this recipe from the NYT (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • Process (1 Minute)
    • 3/4 cup tahini (shake to mix)
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 garlic cloves, diced
    • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    • (Optional) 2-3 Tbsp Harissa, additional for topping
    • Optional Additional Seasonings: Coriander, Caraway, Cayenne Pepper, Smoked Paprika, Chopped Mint, etc.
  • Process (3 Minutes)
    • 2 can chickpea, rinsed and drained
    • (Optional) up to 1/4 cup of water if too thick
  • Optional Toppings
    • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
    • Harissa
    • Parsley
    • Za’atar
    • Roasted Red Peppers

Recipe

  1. In a food process, combine the first set of ingredients until combined, then add the chickpeas and process until smooth
  2. Serve at room temperature. Can serve in a shallow dish and make a small well for the optional toppings
