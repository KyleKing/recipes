Panzanella

Based on https://www.themediterraneandish.com/…

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • Rustic Baguette or 1/2 loaf Italian bread or Ciabatta, cut into 1” cubes
  • 1.5 lbs tomatoes, cubed
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/8 cup Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 tsp Dried Basil or 1/2 cup fresh
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 oz fresh baby mozzarella
  • (Optional) 2 Shallots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • (Optional) 1 cup white beans, such as Peruvian or Navy
  • (Optional) Spinach

Recipe

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400F
    1. In a large mixing bowl, toss the bread cubes, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp thyme, and a generous drizzle of olive oil. Save the bow
    2. Bake on a sheet pan for 10 min and let cool
  2. In a colander over the large bowl, toss the tomatoes with 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp basil, and 1 tsp thyme. Allow to drain for a few minutes
  3. Set the colander aside. With the tomato juice in the bowl, add the red wine vinegar, olive oil, dijon mustard, 1 tsp thyme, 1 tsp basil, garlic cloves, and black pepper. Whisk to combine
  4. In the bowl, combine all ingredients and toss to coat. Let sit for 20-30 minutes
