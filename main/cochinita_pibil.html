Cochinita Pibil

Based on https://www.cilantroparsley.com/cochinita-pibil-instant-pot/

Personal rating: Not yet rated

Ingredients

Pork

  • 3-4 pounds pork shoulder
  • 4 oz achiote paste
  • 6 oranges, freshly squeezed (or 1 cup orange juice)
  • 5 limes, squeezed

Optional Pickled Onion

  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tsp coriander or cumin
  • 2 bay leaves

Garnish

  • Chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Pickled onion

Recipe

  1. Make the sauce: Blend achiote paste, orange juice, and lime juice until smooth. Add to pork and marinate overnight (minimum one hour if time-limited).
  2. Instant Pot method: Place all ingredients in Instant Pot and pressure cook on high for 1 hour.
  3. Oven method: Preheat to 325°F. Line baking dish with three sheets aluminum foil. Add pork with marinade. Cover with three more foil sheets, seal well. Bake 3-4 hours.
  4. Slow cooker method: Add all ingredients. Cook on high 3-4 hours or low 6-7 hours.
  5. Shred the pork: Using a slotted spoon, transfer pork to bowl. Shred with two forks. Pour sauce back over meat.
  6. Optional pickled onions: Simmer onion, water, vinegar, coriander, and bay leaves 2-3 minutes. Cool, refrigerate 1 hour.
  7. Garnish: Top with cilantro, lime wedges, and/or pickled onions.
  8. Serve on rice, tortillas, or gorditas.

Notes

  • Don’t trim fat—it provides essential flavor
  • Seal foil tightly when baking
  • Tastes better the next day as flavors develop
