Guacamole

Based on https://www.mexicoinmykitchen.com/guacamole-recipe (wayback). Previously based on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe avocados, mashed
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup tomatoes, diced
  • Serrano pepper, diced
  • 2 Tbsp cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1.5 limes, juiced
  • Serve with sturdy chips, such as Tostitos

Recipe

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the core, then scoop out the flesh with a spoon in one pass. Mash with a fork until only some chunks remain
  2. Mix in the pico de gallo
