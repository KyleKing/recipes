Chicken Pad See Ew

Based on https://www.cookwell.com/recipe/chicken-pad-see-ew (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Pad See Ew Sauce

  • 25 g Dark soy sauce (1 part)
  • 25 g Soy sauce (1 part)
  • 25 g Oyster sauce (1 part)
  • 25 g Sugar (1 part)
  • 10 g Fish sauce (1/2 part)

Base Stir Fry Ingredients

  • 150 g Wide rice noodles
  • Cooking oil, for stir frying
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 3-4 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • White pepper, to taste
  • 1 Chicken breast, thinly sliced (225 g)
  • 1 bunch Broccolini, roughly chopped (200 g)

Recipe

  1. Prep the noodles and sauce by soaking noodles per package directions. Mix all sauce ingredients until combined; can be prepared ahead.
  2. Stir fry eggs and vegetables by heating wok over high heat with oil. Swirl in beaten egg until fluffy; remove to bowl. Add oil, garlic, and pepper; bloom 30-45 seconds. Stir fry broccolini 1-2 minutes with sauce drizzle; set aside with eggs.
  3. Brown the chicken by adding oil and chicken slices; brown 2 minutes each side. Season with sauce drizzle for color.
  4. Bring it all together by returning broccolini and eggs to wok with drained noodles. Add sauce to coat. Stir fry 5-7 minutes until noodles soften and absorb sauce. Adjust texture with extra sauce or water if needed. Serve with white pepper.

Notes

  • Serves 2-3 in 45 minutes
  • Soaking prevents noodles from stickiness during cooking
  • Cook in stages to achieve proper browning without crowding the pan
  • Taste and adjust seasonings before serving
