Red Cabbage and Lentil Bowl

Based on https://www.loveandlemons.com/buddha-bowl-recipe (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

For the Lentils

  • 1 cup Dried Green or Brown Lentils
  • 2 1/2 cups Water or Vegetable Broth
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

For the Red Cabbage

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 head Red Cabbage, thinly sliced (about 4-5 cups)
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar

For the Bowl

  • 2 cups Cooked Quinoa or Rice
  • 1 large Avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Hummus or Tahini Dressing
  • 2 Tbsp Pumpkin Seeds or Sunflower Seeds
  • (Optional) Fresh Lemon wedges for serving
  • (Optional) Fresh Parsley or Cilantro for garnish

Recipe

  1. Rinse lentils and combine with water and salt in a medium pot
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until tender. Drain any excess liquid
  3. While lentils cook, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  4. Add sliced red cabbage and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened
  5. Add garlic, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1 minute
  6. Stir in balsamic vinegar and cook for another 2 minutes. Remove from heat
  7. Assemble bowls with quinoa or rice as the base
  8. Top with cooked lentils, sautéed red cabbage, sliced avocado, and a dollop of hummus
  9. Sprinkle with seeds and garnish with fresh herbs
  10. Serve with lemon wedges for squeezing over the top

Notes

  • Can substitute chickpeas for lentils if preferred
  • Add roasted sweet potatoes or butternut squash for extra heartiness
  • Make components ahead and assemble bowls throughout the week
  • For added protein, top with a fried or poached egg
  • Tahini dressing recipe: Mix 1/4 cup tahini with 2 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 Tbsp maple syrup, water to thin, and salt to taste
