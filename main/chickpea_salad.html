Chickpea Salad

Based on https://downshiftology.com/recipes/mediterranean-chickpea-salad

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 2 cans Chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • Cucumber, large dice
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, large dice
  • 2 cups Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion, diced
  • 4 ounces Feta Cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Parsely, finely chopped
  • (Optional) Avocado, medium diced
  • 1 Tbsp Lemon Vinaigrette or Olive Oil and Lemon Juice, to taste

Recipe

  1. Prep and toss all ingredients in a bowl
