Chickpea Salad

Based on https://downshiftology.com/recipes/mediterranean-chickpea-salad (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) Chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large Cucumber, large dice
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, large dice
  • 2 cups Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion, diced
  • 4 ounces Feta Cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Parsley, finely chopped
  • (Optional) Avocado, medium diced

Dressing

  • 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Recipe

  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper
  2. Prep vegetables: Dice cucumber and bell pepper, halve tomatoes, dice onion, chop parsley
  3. Combine: In a large bowl, add chickpeas and all vegetables. Pour dressing over and toss well
  4. Top with crumbled feta and optional avocado. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 30 minutes to let flavors meld

Notes

  1. For meal prep: Store dressing separately and add just before serving to keep vegetables crisp
  2. Add protein: Top with grilled chicken or hard-boiled eggs for a complete meal
