Bruschetta

Loosely based on https://www.geniuskitchen.com/recipe/…

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 2 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Basil
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Recipe

  1. Blend in mini food processor or mince and combine
  2. Variations
    1. For a meal, serve on toast with poached egg. Top with oregano
    2. Serve on sliced baguette crisps. Add parmesan cheese
