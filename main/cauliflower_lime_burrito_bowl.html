Cauliflower Lime Burrito Bowl

Based on https://www.mealime.com/recipes/… with an olive oil and spice l-cilantro-lime-rice/7720. Cauliflower in Instant Pot inspired by https://www.pressurecookrecipes.com/instant-pot-cauliflower/…

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

Bowl

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 1 cup basmati rice, quinoa, etc.
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed
  • 1 (15 oz) can corn, rinsed

Chili Powder Spices

Previously included 1 Tbsp chili powder and 1/2 tsp of the rest, but I calculated an approximate substitute based on: https://www.spendwithpennies.com/homemade-chili-powde/…

  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano

Toppings

Note: this serves 4, so save one of each for the 2nd day

  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 2 limes, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small bunch cilantro, remove and mince the leaves
  • (Optional) salsa

Recipe

  1. Pour 1 cup of water and place the trivet in the Instant Pot
    1. Remove most of the leaves from the Cauliflower
    2. Cook for “0” min at high pressure (if longer, will be too soft)
    3. Once at pressure, quick release
    4. Remove the cauliflower to cool
  2. Prep the spice mix
  3. In a large skillet, on medium heat, saute the onion with half of the spices
  4. Rinse the rice and store in 2-cup measure (and save strainer for beans and corn)
    1. In the Instant Pot, combine rice and water at a 1:1.5 ratio with broth or water
    2. Cook for 8 min at high pressure
    3. Quick release
    4. Add juice of 1 lime per cup of rice and fluff
  5. Add the beans and corn to the pan
  6. Once cool enough, remove the remaining leaves, chop the cauliflower into bite size pieces, and add to the pan
    1. Coat with remaining spices
  7. Serve in bowls with diced toppings

Notes

  1. The cauliflower can also be roasted at >425F. Chop into bite size pieces, coat with olive oil and half of spices, then roast for 20 minutes
