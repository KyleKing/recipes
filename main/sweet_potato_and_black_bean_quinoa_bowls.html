Sweet Potato and Black Bean Quinoa Bowls

Based on https://www.spoonfulofflavor.com/sweet-potato-black-bean-quinoa-bowls/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

Roasted Sweet Potato:

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt

Cilantro Cream Drizzle:

  • 1/4 cup plain non fat Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp agave nectar or honey
  • Juice of half a lime
  • Pinch of salt, garlic powder, and chili powder

Quinoa:

  • 1.5 cups red quinoa
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tsp kosher salt, divided
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Juice of half a lime
  • 3 Tbsp cilantro, chopped

Other Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Cilantro for garnishing

Recipe

  1. Start the quinoa in a rice cooker
  2. Roast the Sweet Potato:
    1. Preheat oven to 425F. Prepare a large baking sheet with a baking mat, foil, or cooking spray
    2. Toss the diced sweet potato in the olive oil and spices. Spread out in an even layer on the baking sheet
    3. Roast the sweet potato for 12-15 minutes or until it is fork tender
  3. For the Cilantro Cream Drizzle, in a blender, add all of the ingredients and blend until smooth
    1. Easier sauce alternatives: Plain Greek yogurt with lime juice, store-bought cilantro lime dressing, or simply drizzle with lime juice and olive oil
  4. Once the quinoa is done, season with the specified spices
  5. Assembling the Bowls:
    1. In 2 or 3 serving bowls divide the quinoa mixture
    2. Top the quinoa with the black beans and roasted sweet potato
    3. Drizzle the cilantro cream on top with a spoon. Top with more cilantro if desired.
