Breakfast Tacos

Based on https://www.loveandlemons.com/breakfast-tacos (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

No image yet

Ingredients

Per Two Tacos

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 Avocado, sliced
  • 2 tortillas
  • (Optional) Cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Pico de Gallo, Salsa, Diced Tomatoes, etc.
  • Cilantro
  • Hot sauce

Recipe

  1. In either a skillet or microwave (30s + 10s as needed), cook the eggs
  2. Heat the tortillas (can also be done in microwave between two plates)
  3. Serve with choice of toppings
