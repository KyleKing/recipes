Tomato Mozzarella Caprese

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • tomato, sliced
  • mozzarella ball, sliced
  • quality extra virgin olive oil
  • basil
  • oregano
  • ground pepper
  • baguette

Recipe

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella. Drizzle olive oil on top with a small amount of balsamic vinegar
  2. For the bread, add a bowl of ground pepper and olive oil for dipping
