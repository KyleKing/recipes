Caesar Salad

Based on https://www.seriouseats.com/caesar-salad-recipe (wayback unavailable)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Salad

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shaved or grated
  • 1.5 cups croutons
  • (Optional) Grilled chicken breast, sliced

Recipe

  1. Make the dressing: In a medium bowl, whisk together garlic, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce. Add mayonnaise and Parmesan, whisk until smooth. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Prep the lettuce: Wash and dry romaine thoroughly. Chop into bite-sized pieces
  3. Assemble: In a large bowl, toss romaine with dressing until well coated. Add croutons and Parmesan, toss gently
  4. Serve immediately

Notes

  1. For healthier version: Use Greek yogurt instead of mayonnaise, or reduce dressing amount
  2. Make-ahead: Dressing can be refrigerated for up to 3 days
  3. For croutons: Cube bread, toss with olive oil and garlic powder, bake at 375F for 10-15 minutes until golden
  4. Add protein: Top with grilled chicken, salmon, or shrimp for a complete meal
