Crispy Tofu Fingers

Based on easygfrecipes.com

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 12 oz extra-firm tofu
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 4 oz sweet and sour sauce or barbecue sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, or cayenne pepper (optional)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Apply vegetable oil to a cookie sheet.
  2. Slice tofu lengthwise into strips approximately 1/4 inch thick.
  3. Wrap tofu strips in paper towels and press under a heavy object for at least 30 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  4. Pour sauce into a shallow dish and coat each tofu strip thoroughly on all sides.
  5. Arrange sauce-coated strips on the prepared sheet in a single layer without overlapping.
  6. Season with salt and any desired seasonings.
  7. Bake for 20-30 minutes until golden brown and crispy around the edges. Optionally broil for a final 1-2 minutes for extra crispness.
  8. Cool briefly before serving.

Notes

  • Pressing is essential - the more moisture removed, the crispier the result; use a tofu press for best results
  • Air fryer alternative: 375°F for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway
  • Store leftovers up to 3 days refrigerated; reheat in oven or air fryer to maintain texture
