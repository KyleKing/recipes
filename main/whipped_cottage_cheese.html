Whipped Cottage Cheese

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove (or 1/4 tsp garlic powder)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Recipe

  1. Blend all ingredients until completely smooth, about 1–2 minutes
  2. Taste and adjust lemon, garlic, and salt
  3. Transfer to a bowl and top as desired

Notes

Topping Ideas

  • Olive oil + red pepper flakes + fresh herbs (parsley, chives)
  • Everything bagel seasoning
  • Roasted tomatoes or sun-dried tomatoes
  • Za’atar + olive oil
  • Honey + walnuts (sweet direction)

Uses

  • Dip for vegetables, pita, or crackers — works as a hummus alternative
  • Spread on toast
  • Base for a savory bowl (see cottagecheesesavory_bowl)
  • Dolloped onto shakshuka or grain bowls
