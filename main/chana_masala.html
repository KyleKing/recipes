Chana Masala

Based on https://twosleevers.com/pressure-cooker-homestyle-chana-masala/ (wayback) and https://ministryofcurry.com/instant-pot-chana-masala-recipe/ (wayback)

Ingredients

Chickpeas

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight (or 2x 15 oz cans, drained)
  • 2.5 cups water (if using dried chickpeas)

Sauce Base

  • 1 Tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 1 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, minced (or 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup crushed tomatoes)
  • 2 tsp chana masala spice blend (or substitute: 1 tsp garam masala + 1/2 tsp ground coriander + 1/2 tsp ground cumin)
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt

Finishing

  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice (or 1 tsp amchoor/dried mango powder)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

For Serving

  • Basmati rice or naan bread

Recipe

  1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight in plenty of water; drain before using. For canned chickpeas, skip to step 4
  2. Add soaked chickpeas and 2.5 cups water to Instant Pot
  3. Close lid and cook on high pressure for 15-20 minutes; allow natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure
  4. Open lid and drain excess liquid, reserving 1 cup of cooking liquid
  5. Set Instant Pot to sauté mode and heat ghee or oil
  6. Add cumin seeds and let sizzle for 30 seconds until fragrant
  7. Add diced onion and cook until softened and golden, about 5-7 minutes
  8. Add ginger, garlic, and green chili; cook for 1 minute until fragrant
  9. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes
  10. Add chana masala spice blend, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and salt; stir and cook for 1 minute
  11. Return cooked chickpeas to pot with 1/2-1 cup of reserved cooking liquid
  12. Use back of spoon or potato masher to lightly mash some chickpeas to thicken sauce
  13. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until sauce thickens and chickpeas are well-coated
  14. Turn off heat and stir in lemon juice and garam masala
  15. Serve garnished with fresh cilantro, alongside rice or naan

Notes

  • Serves 4
  • Total time: 50 minutes (30 min for chickpeas + 20 min for sauce) plus overnight soaking
  • For quick soak: Cover dried chickpeas with boiling water and soak 2 hours
  • Using canned chickpeas reduces cook time to 20 minutes
  • Vegetarian and can easily be made vegan by using oil instead of ghee
  • High in protein (11g per serving) and fiber (14g per serving)
  • Nutrition per serving: 260 calories, 34g carbs
  • Chana masala spice blend is available at most US grocery stores in the international aisle
  • The slight tanginess from lemon juice or amchoor is essential to authentic flavor
