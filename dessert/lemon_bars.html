Lemon Bars with Shortbread Crust

Based on Sally’s Baking Addiction

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup (16 Tbsp; 226g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups + 2 Tbsps (265g) all-purpose flour

Filling

  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 6 Tbsps (46g) all-purpose flour
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 2 tsp fresh lemon zest (optional)
  • Confectioners’ sugar for dusting (optional)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Line a 9x13-inch glass baking pan with parchment paper with overhang for easy removal.
  2. Mix melted butter, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Stir in flour until combined. Press firmly into prepared pan. Bake 20-22 minutes until edges brown lightly. Using a fork, poke holes across the warm crust surface to help filling adhere.
  3. Sift sugar and flour together. Whisk in eggs, then lemon juice and zest until fully combined.
  4. Pour filling over warm crust. Bake 22-26 minutes until center is set with minimal jiggle. Cool completely at room temperature (about 2 hours), then refrigerate 1-2 hours until chilled.
  5. Lift parchment from pan using overhang. Dust with confectioners’ sugar and cut into squares. Wipe knife between cuts for neat edges.

Notes

  • Store leftovers refrigerated up to 1 week.
  • Freeze cooled, cut bars (without sugar) for 3-4 months by freezing on a sheet first, then wrapping individually.
