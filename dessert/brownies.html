Brownies

Based on https://tasty.co/recipe/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, 2 sticks
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate bar, coarsely chopped
  • 1.5 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp espresso powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Add the butter and chopped chocolate bar to a large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in 15-second increments, stirring between each, until smooth. Let sit for 3 minutes to cool slightly
  3. Add the granulated and brown sugars, vanilla, espresso powder, and salt. Whisk to combine
  4. Whisk in the eggs one at a time until fully combined and glossy
  5. Sift the flour and cocoa powder into the bowl and fold in with a spatula until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips
  6. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan. Spread the batter evenly in the pan
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached
  8. Let cool completely before cutting

Notes

  • For fudgier texture and shiny crust: heat butter and sugar together to 110-120F before mixing with eggs (technique from King Arthur Baking (wayback))
