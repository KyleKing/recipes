Peanut Butter Cupcakes

Based on https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/peanut-butter-cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 1.25 cups all-purpose flour, spooned & leveled
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup canola or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup sour cream, at room temperature
  • 1.5 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup very finely crushed or chopped peanuts

Chocolate Peanut Butter Frosting

  • 1 cup (16 Tbsps) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3.5 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 Tbsps heavy cream or milk
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Optional: mini Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups or chopped peanuts for garnish

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan with cupcake liners. Add 2-3 liners to a second pan since this recipe yields 14-15 cupcakes.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl; set aside.
  3. Using a handheld or stand mixer, combine oil, peanut butter, brown sugar, egg, sour cream, and vanilla. Add dry ingredients, milk, and peanuts; mix until combined without overmixing.
  4. Fill liners two-thirds full. Bake 21-23 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean and tops spring back when touched. (Mini cupcakes: bake 11-13 minutes.) Cool completely before frosting.
  5. Beat softened butter on medium speed until creamy, about 2 minutes.
  6. Add confectioners’ sugar, cocoa powder, and milk; beat on low for 30 seconds.
  7. Add peanut butter, vanilla, and salt; beat on medium-high for 2 minutes until combined.
  8. Adjust consistency with more confectioners’ sugar if too thin or cream/milk if too thick.
  9. Frost cooled cupcakes and garnish with peanut butter cups or chopped peanuts if desired.

Notes

  • Store leftovers covered in the refrigerator for up to 5 days
  • A cupcake carrier is recommended for storing and transporting decorated cupcakes
