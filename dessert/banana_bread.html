Banana Bread

Source: Joy of Cooking 75th edition, page 628. Banana Bread Cockaigne (Note: removed the lemon zest from the recipe)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1.5 cups Flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 6 Tbsp butter, softened

Beat-In

  • 1-2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1-1.25 cups bananas (about 3 medium or 2 large, ripe bananas), mashed

Fold In

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped dried apricots (optional)
  • etc (optional)

Recipe

  1. Have all ingredients at room temperature. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease an 8.5 x 4.5 in loaf pan
  2. Whisk together the dry ingredients in a medium bowl
  3. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter at medium speed until light and fluffy (about 2-3 minutes)
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then mix in the mashed bananas until well combined
  5. Add the dry ingredients in 3 parts, mixing on low speed just until combined after each addition. Do not overmix
  6. Gently fold in optional ingredients (chocolate chips, chopped nuts, etc.) with a spatula
  7. Scrape batter into the greased pan, smooth the top, and bake for 55-65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean or with just a few moist crumbs
  8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely

Notes

Healthier Variations

  • Reduce sugar: Use 1/2 cup sugar instead of 2/3 cup. The ripe bananas provide natural sweetness
  • Whole grain: Replace half or all of the all-purpose flour with whole wheat flour or white whole wheat flour
  • Healthier fats: Substitute butter with equal amount of coconut oil, olive oil, or unsweetened applesauce (though texture will be slightly different)
  • Boost nutrition: Add 1-2 Tbsp ground flaxseed or chia seeds, or swap 1/4 cup flour for oat flour
  • Natural sweetness: Skip chocolate chips and increase dried fruit (dates, figs, or raisins work well)
  • Greek yogurt version: Replace 3 Tbsp butter with 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt for added protein and moisture
  • Reduce portion size: Bake in a muffin tin instead (makes 12 muffins, bake 20-25 minutes)
