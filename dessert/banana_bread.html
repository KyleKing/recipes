Banana Bread

Source: Joy of Cooking 75th edition, page 628. Banana Bread Cockaigne (Note: removed the lemon zest from the recipe)

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1.5 cups Flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 6 Tbsp butter, softened

Beat-In

  • 1-2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1-1.25 cups bananas (about 3 medium or 2 large, ripe bananas), mashed

Fold In

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped dried apricots (optional)
  • etc (optional)

Recipe

  1. Have all ingredients at room temperature. Preheat the oven to 350. Grease an 8.5 x 4.5 in loaf pan
  2. Whisk together the dry ingredients
  3. In a large bowl, beat at medium speed the sugar and butter until creamy
  4. Beat in the eggs and ripe bananas
  5. In about 3 parts, add the dry ingredients and beat until smooth
  6. Fold in optional ingredients (chocolate chips, chopped nuts, etc.)
  7. Scrape batter into greased pan and bake about 1 hour or until inserted toothpick comes out clean
