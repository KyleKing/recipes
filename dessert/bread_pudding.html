Bread Pudding

Based on https://www.allrecipes.com/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Baguette or other stale bread, small pieces
  • 2 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1.5 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In 8” square pan, place bread scraps and sprinkle butter over top
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk milk, sugar, eggs, cinnamon, and vanilla until well combined
  4. Pour in the mixture and use a fork to make sure the bread is submerged
  5. Bake until golden brown and the top springs back, about 45 min

Notes

  • Goes great with Horchata Ice Cream!
  • Consider chocolate variations, adding chocolate chips or nutella for serving, etc.
