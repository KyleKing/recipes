Super Moist Chocolate Cupcakes

Based on Sally’s Baking Addiction

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup (94g) all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
  • 1/2 cup (41g) unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp espresso powder or instant espresso
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) packed light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) vegetable or canola oil (or melted coconut oil)
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) buttermilk, at room temperature
  • Chocolate buttercream and sprinkles for decorating

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line a 12-count muffin pan with cupcake liners and a second pan with 2 liners (recipe yields about 14 cupcakes).
  2. Whisk flour, cocoa powder, espresso powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs, sugars, oil, and vanilla until smooth. Combine wet and dry ingredients alternately with buttermilk, stirring gently until just combined—do not overmix.
  3. Fill liners only halfway (critical to prevent overflow). Bake 18-21 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely before frosting.
  4. Frost with chocolate buttercream.

Notes

  • Store covered in the refrigerator up to 3 days.
