Biscotti

Based on https://www.kingarthurflour.com/recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp butter
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups chocolate chips, cranberries
  • (Optional) 1 Tbsp cocoa powder

Recipe

  1. In the large glass or metal bowl, beat the butter, sugar, salt, vanilla, and baking powder until the mixture is smooth and creamy
  2. Beat in the eggs and add the flour until smooth. Will be sticky
  3. Add the chocolate chips and stir until uniform
  4. Preheat oven to 350
    1. Place parchment paper on or lightly grease a baking sheet
    2. Divide the dough in half and with wet hands, shape into two logs 2 in wide, 10 in long, and about 1 in thick
    3. Bake for 25-30 minutes, remove
  5. Allow to cool for 30 minutes, then carefully transfer to a cutting board
  6. Preheat oven to 325
    1. Cover logs with a damp towel and wait another ~5 min before using a serrated knife to diagonally slice into 1/2” - 3/4” wide biscotti
    2. Make sure to saw the knife back and forth. Putting too much vertical pressure with the knife can cause the biscotti to crumble
    3. Return the slices to the baking sheet, standing them up with 1/2” space between them so that air can circulate
    4. Return the biscotti to the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, until they feel very dry and are beginning to turn golden
  7. Transfer to a rack to cool

Notes

  1. The King Arthur website has a number of tasty recipes like chocolate dipped biscotti
