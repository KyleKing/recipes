Popcorn Cinnamon Sugar

Personal rating: 2 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kernels
  • ~1 Tbsp butter
  • ~1 tsp cinnamon
  • ~2 tsp sugar

Recipe

  1. Place in popper and cook for 2:20 in our 900 watt microwave
  2. Microwave the butter until softened then mix with the popcorn. Coat with the cinnamon and sugar

Notes

  1. Measurements are all estimates
