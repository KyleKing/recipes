Soft & Chewy Chocolate Snickerdoodles

Based on https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/chocolate-snickerdoodles

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 2.67 cups all-purpose flour, spooned & leveled
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (16 Tbsps) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Topping

  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Recipe

  1. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder, cream of tartar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt; set aside.
  2. Beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy (about 3 minutes). Add eggs and vanilla; beat until combined.
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients; beat on low speed until incorporated.
  4. Cover and chill dough at least 2 hours (or up to 3 days). If chilling longer, let sit 20 minutes at room temperature before scooping.
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. Mix granulated sugar and cinnamon for topping.
  7. Scoop dough into 1.5 Tbsp (35g) balls; roll in cinnamon-sugar. Arrange 2-3 inches apart on baking sheets.
  8. Bake until edges are set but centers look puffy (13-15 minutes). Cool on sheet 10 minutes, then transfer to cooling rack.

Notes

  • Cream of tartar provides signature tang and soft texture—not recommended to omit
  • Cookies will slightly deflate as they cool
  • For crackly tops: ensure well-chilled dough and proper bake time so cookies puff in the oven
  • Store in airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days
