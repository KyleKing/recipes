Easy Key Lime Pie Bars

Based on Sally’s Baking Addiction

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 12 full-sheet graham crackers (yields 1.5 cups/180g crumbs)
  • 6 Tbsps (85g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 4 ounces (113g) full-fat brick cream cheese, softened
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 14-ounce (397g) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) key lime juice
  • 2 tsp lime zest (about 1 lime or 2 key limes)
  • Optional garnish: whipped cream and lime slices

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line 9-inch square pan with parchment paper, leaving overhang for easy removal.
  2. Grind graham crackers into fine crumbs. Mix crumbs with sugar, then stir in melted butter. Press mixture into pan bottom.
  3. Prebake crust for 10 minutes, then cool briefly.
  4. Beat softened cream cheese until smooth. Add egg yolks and beat. Mix in condensed milk, key lime juice, and lime zest. Hand-whisk to remove any lumps, then pour over warm crust.
  5. Bake 18-20 minutes until edges are set and center is slightly jiggly.
  6. Cool 30 minutes at room temperature, then refrigerate at least 2-3 hours.
  7. Lift parchment from pan and cut into squares. Garnish with whipped cream, lime slices, or zest.

Notes

  • Keeps up to 5 days in airtight refrigerated container.
