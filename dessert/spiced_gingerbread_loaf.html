Spiced Gingerbread Loaf

Based on Sally’s Baking Addiction

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp ground ginger
  • 1.5 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2/3 cup (226g) unsulphured or dark molasses
  • 3/4 cup (180g/ml) hot water (approximately 100°F)
  • 1/2 cup (8 Tbsp; 113g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup (67g) packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) confectioners’ sugar, sifted
  • 2-3 Tbsps (30-45g/ml) orange juice

Recipe

  1. Position oven rack to lower third; preheat to 350°F (177°C). Grease an 8x4-inch or 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk molasses and hot water together.
  4. Beat softened butter and brown sugar on medium-high speed for about 3 minutes. Add egg and vanilla; beat until combined. Alternately add dry ingredients and molasses mixture in three additions, mixing until just incorporated. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  5. Bake 50-60 minutes (55-65 minutes for smaller pans) until a toothpick inserted in the center emerges clean with just a few moist crumbs.
  6. Cool in pan for 1 hour, then remove to cooling rack until completely cooled.
  7. Whisk confectioners’ sugar with orange juice until smooth; drizzle over cooled loaf.

Notes

  • Store covered in refrigerator up to 5 days.
