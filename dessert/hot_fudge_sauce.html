Hot Fudge Sauce

Alex’s Family recipe

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 2 oz unsweetened chocolate
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1/3 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Recipe

  1. Melt butter and chocolate over low heat. Stir and blend well, then add the boiling water
  2. Stir and blend well, then add the sugar and corn syrup
  3. Let the sauce come to a boil, stirring slowly over low heat. Simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Add vanilla

Notes

  1. If using Cocoa powder, for every 1 oz chocolate, use 3 Tbsp cocoa powder + 1 Tbsp butter
