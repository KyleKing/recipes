Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes

Based on https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/lemon-blueberry-cupcakes

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (8 Tbsps) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1.5 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk or buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries, tossed in 1 Tbsp flour

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 ounces full-fat brick cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1/4 cup (4 Tbsps) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 2 cups confectioners’ sugar
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch salt
  • Optional garnish: lemon slices and extra blueberries

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan with cupcake liners. This recipe yields approximately 15 cupcakes.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. Using a handheld or stand mixer with a paddle attachment, beat butter, sugar, and lemon zest on medium-high speed until creamed (about 3 minutes). Scrape bowl sides as needed. Add eggs and vanilla extract, beating about 1 minute. Add dry ingredients on low speed, slowly pour in milk and lemon juice, and beat until just combined. Fold in the flour-coated blueberries without overmixing.
  4. Pour batter into liners—fill only 2/3 full. Bake 18-21 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. (Mini cupcakes: bake 11-13 minutes at same temperature.) Cool completely before frosting.
  5. Beat cream cheese and butter together on medium speed until smooth (about 2 minutes). Add confectioners’ sugar, vanilla, and salt; beat on low for 30 seconds, then increase to medium-high for 2 full minutes. Taste and adjust salt if desired. Refrigerate at least 20 minutes for intricate piping.
  6. Frost cooled cupcakes and add optional garnish. Refrigerate decorated cupcakes uncovered for at least 20 minutes before serving.

Notes

  • Cover and store leftover cupcakes in the refrigerator for up to 5 days
