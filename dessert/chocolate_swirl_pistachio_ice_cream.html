Chocolate Swirl Pistachio Ice Cream

Based on https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/chocolate-swirl-pistachio-ice-cream

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Chocolate Ganache

  • 6 Tbsps heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup quality chocolate chips

Pistachio Ice Cream

  • 1.25 cups shelled roasted pistachios
  • 2 cups heavy cream, cold
  • 14-ounce can full-fat sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract or vanilla bean paste
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract
  • Optional: tiny drop green gel food coloring

Topping

  • 2 Tbsps roughly chopped pistachios
  • 2 Tbsps roughly chopped chocolate chips

Recipe

  1. Heat cream until simmering, pour over chocolate chips, let sit 2-3 minutes, then stir until melted. Set aside to cool and thicken.
  2. Pulse pistachios in food processor until finely ground.
  3. Beat cold heavy cream on medium-high speed until stiff peaks form (about 4 minutes).
  4. Add ground pistachios, condensed milk, vanilla, almond extract, and food coloring if desired. Beat on low speed until combined.
  5. Spread half the ice cream mixture into a freezer-safe container, add half the ganache in spoonfuls, top with remaining ice cream, then remaining ganache. Swirl gently with a knife. Top with chopped pistachios and chocolate.
  6. Cover and freeze at least 8 hours (up to 2 weeks).

Notes

  • This “no-churn method” doesn’t require an ice cream maker
