Oatmeal Balls

Based on https://www.blessthismessplease.com/8-no-bake-oatmeal-energy-balls/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 3 / 5

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats (quick or old fashioned)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (mini or normal)
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup (or honey)
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder (optional)

Recipe

  1. Combine all ingredients into one bowl, form into balls, and freeze
