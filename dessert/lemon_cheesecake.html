Lemon Cheesecake

Based on Sally’s Baking Addiction

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1.75 cups (210g) graham cracker crumbs (about 14 full-sheet graham crackers)
  • 5 Tbsps (71g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

Filling

  • 1.25 cups (250g) granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp packed lemon zest (about 1 lemon)
  • 32 ounces (904g) full-fat brick cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) fresh lemon juice (about 3-4 lemons), at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup (80g) sour cream or plain yogurt, at room temperature
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature

Topping

  • 3/4 cup lemon curd (highly recommended)
  • Homemade whipped cream
  • Lemon slices and/or fresh berries

Recipe

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower-middle position; preheat to 350°F (177°C).
  2. Process graham crackers into fine crumbs. Mix with sugar, then melted butter until sandy. Press firmly into bottom and slightly up sides of ungreased 9-inch springform pan. Pre-bake 10 minutes. Place hot pan on aluminum foil for water bath.
  3. Place sugar in food processor; add lemon zest on top. Pulse until blended, about 10-12 pulses.
  4. Using mixer with paddle attachment, beat cream cheese and lemon sugar on medium-high speed until smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes. Add lemon juice, sour cream, and vanilla; beat until combined. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each until just blended. Avoid over-mixing.
  5. Pour batter into warm crust; smooth into even layer.
  6. Boil water (need 1 inch in roasting pan). Place springform pan inside large roasting pan; carefully pour hot water around it.
  7. Optional: Make lemon curd on stovetop as cheesecake bakes.
  8. Bake 55-70 minutes until center is almost set. Center should slightly wobble when pan is gently tapped. Turn oven off, open door slightly, and let cheesecake sit in water bath as it cools for 1 hour.
  9. Remove from oven and water bath; cool completely at room temperature uncovered.
  10. Spread approximately 3/4 cup lemon curd on cooled cheesecake near edges without spilling over. Use offset spatula.
  11. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.
  12. Loosen cheesecake from springform rim with knife; remove rim. Add whipped cream, lemon slices, and berries as desired. Using clean sharp knife dipped in warm water between cuts, slice for serving.

Notes

  • Cover and refrigerate leftovers for up to 5 days.
