Baked Apples

Based on https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/… (wayback)

Personal rating: 2 / 5

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 4 large red baking apples (2 apples)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (2 Tbsp)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (1/2 tsp)
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans, option (2 Tbsp)
  • 1/4 cup chopped raisins (2 Tbsp)
  • 1/4 Tbsp butter per apple
  • 3/4 cup boiling water (3/4 cup)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Core the apples leaving the bottom 1/2 inch intact
  3. Place the brown sugar, cinnamon, chopped raisins, and chopped pecans (if using) in a small bowl and stir to combine. Place the apples in a baking dish and fill with the mixture
  4. On each apple, place a 1/4 Tbsp of butter on top of the sugar
  5. Pour the boiling water into the bottom of the baking dish. Bake for 30-45 minutes, until tender but not overcooked and mushy. When done, baste with juices from the pan

Notes

  1. Try to look for Rome Beauty, Golden Delicious, or Jonagold
