Red Lentil Caldo with Mexican Spices

Adapted from lentils.md research document

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Optional: 1 Tbsp olive oil for sauteing
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced (or 1 can diced tomatoes, 14 oz)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric (optional)
  • Optional: 1 dried guajillo chile, seeded
  • 4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving

  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil drizzle
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Crusty bread or tortillas

Recipe

  1. (Optional) Saute onion and garlic in olive oil in Instant Pot on saute mode for 2-3 minutes
  2. Add red lentils, carrots, tomatoes, spices, guajillo chile (if using), and broth
  3. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes
  4. Quick release pressure
  5. Optional: Blend partially or fully for creamy texture (remove chile first)
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve with lime juice, olive oil drizzle, and cilantro

Notes

  • Red lentils cook quickly and break down naturally, creating a creamy texture without dairy; their nutty flavor complements the Mexican spices
  • Very digestible for kids and toddlers
  • Excellent iron and vitamin A combination from lentils and carrots
  • For thicker soup, use less broth or simmer on saute mode after cooking
  • For thinner soup, add more broth after pressure cooking
  • Guajillo chile adds mild heat and depth; omit for completely mild version
