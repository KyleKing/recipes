Parmesan Minestrone

Based on Chrissy Teigen Cravings Hungry for More (wayback), pg. 50

Personal rating: 5 / 5

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, cut into 1/2” dice
  • 6 cloves garlic (3 tsp), diced
  • 4 medium carrots, halved and coined
  • 2 celery sticks or a green pepper, cut into 1/2” dice
  • 3-4 cups vegetable or chicken broth. Add more broth or water as needed
  • 1 (28-oz) can of diced tomatoes (keep juice) or ~1 lb (4-5 medium) fresh tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 (15-oz) can white beans (cannellini/navy, peruvian, pinto, etc.), drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15-oz) can (keep juice), 1/2 bag of frozen, or fresh green beans (trimmed and cut into 1.5” pieces)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2/3 cup pasta wheels (~1/2 cup elbow/macaroni)
  • 1 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, plus more for garnish
  • Serve with baguette or toast

Recipe

  1. In a large ceramic dutch oven, heat the oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook ~8 min until tender. Add garlic and cook 1 additional minute
  2. Add the carrots and celery and cook ~4 min until slightly softened
  3. Add the broth, the three canned ingredients, and spices. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer and cook for 20 min until thickened
  4. Add the pasta and cook until tender for another ~10 min
  5. Stir in the Parmesan and season with additional salt and black pepper.

Notes

  • If using dry beans, see no-soak recipe
