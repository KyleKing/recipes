Lentil Soup

Based on https://www.thepioneerwoman.com/food-cooking/…

Personal rating: 4 / 5

Ingredients

In Instant Pot

  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3-5 pieces of celery, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1.5 cups dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 7 cups vegetable broth
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1.5 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Added Last

  • 8 oz baby spinach
  • Parmesan, for serving
  • Crusty bread, for serving

Recipe

  1. In an Instant Pot, combine the ingredients
  2. Select high pressure for 10 minutes
  3. Quick release and remove the bay leaf
  4. Stir in the spinach and let wilt
  5. Serve the soup with grated Parmesan, fresh black pepper, and side of bread
