Chocolate Date Energy Balls

Based on Chocolate Date Energy Balls - JoyFoodSunshine (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup raw pecans
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Recipe

  1. Line a baking pan with wax paper for storing rolled bites.
  2. Combine dates, pecans, and cashews in a food processor fitted with an “S” blade. Process until the mixture becomes fine and uniform throughout.
  3. Add cocoa powder, salt, almond butter, almond milk, and vanilla to the processor. Blend until the mixture just starts to clump together and holds when squeezed.
  4. Measure 1 tablespoon portions of the mixture and roll each into a ball. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Transfer the rolled energy balls to an airtight container and refrigerate or freeze until chilled and hardened.
  6. Serve cold or at room temperature.

Notes

  1. Makes 20 energy bites
  2. Suggested serving size: 2-3 balls for a satisfying sweet treat
  3. Refrigerate up to 2 weeks or freeze up to 3 months
  4. Dates provide natural sweetness and fiber, nuts add protein and healthy fats
