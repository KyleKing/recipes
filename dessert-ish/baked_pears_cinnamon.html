Baked Pears with Cinnamon

Based on Healthy Baked Pears with Cinnamon - Running on Real Food (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

  • Ripe pears (as many as desired)
  • 1/2 tsp maple syrup per half pear
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon per half pear

Optional Toppings

  • Coconut yogurt
  • Nuts
  • Granola
  • Whipped cream

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Slice pears in half and gently scoop out seeds using a small cookie scoop or spoon.
  3. Place pear halves cut-side up on a baking pan or oven-proof dish.
  4. Drizzle each pear half with 1/2 tsp maple syrup and sprinkle with 1/4 tsp cinnamon.
  5. Bake until soft and tender, approximately 25-30 minutes.
  6. Serve warm with desired toppings.

Notes

  1. Anjou and Bosc varieties are top picks for baking as they hold their shape and texture best
  2. Suggested serving size: 1 pear half
  3. Per serving (1 pear half): 63 calories, 15g carbs, 0.5g protein, 3.5g fiber
  4. Leftovers can be refrigerated for 2-3 days and served cold over waffles, pancakes, or oatmeal
  5. Minimal added sugar - relies on natural sweetness of pears
  6. Serves 4 pear halves
