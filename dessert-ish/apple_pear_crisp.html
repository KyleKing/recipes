Apple Pear Crisp

Based on Rustic Apple & Pear Crisp - The Simple Veganista (wayback)

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Ingredients

Filling

  • 3 medium apples, cored and chopped
  • 3 medium pears, cored and chopped
  • 3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp sugar (coconut or organic cane)
  • 1 heaping tsp cinnamon

Topping

  • 1 3/4 cups old fashioned oats
  • 1/4 cup almond meal or flaxseed meal (optional)
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 3-4 Tbsp coconut oil (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp mineral salt
  • Cinnamon for garnish

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Wash, core, and dice the apples and pears.
  3. Combine fruit with lemon juice, sugar, and cinnamon in a 9x9 baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix oats, almond meal, walnuts, maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla, and salt. Spread over fruit.
  5. Sprinkle with additional cinnamon for garnish.
  6. Bake 35-40 minutes until fruit is fork-tender.
  7. Cool 5-10 minutes before serving.

Notes

  1. Serves 6 generously in about 50 minutes total
  2. Suggested serving size: 1/6 of the crisp (about 3/4 cup)
  3. Per serving: 344 calories, 4.4g protein, 22.1g fat, 36g carbs, 4.9g fiber
  4. Uses natural sweeteners (maple syrup) and minimal added sugar compared to traditional crisps
  5. Vegan and can be made gluten-free by using certified gluten-free oats
