Coffee & Cardamom Bites

Personal rating: Not yet rated

No image yet

Bitter espresso and warm cardamom carry most of the flavor — honey plays a supporting role. Pairs well with an afternoon coffee. Strongly adult-leaning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 3 Tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp espresso powder (or finely ground instant coffee)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp mini dark chocolate chips (optional; complements the coffee flavor)
  • 2 Tbsp ground flaxseed or chia seeds (optional; improves binding)

Recipe

  1. Mix almond butter and honey until smooth.
  2. Stir in oats, espresso powder, cardamom, salt, and any optional add-ins until a uniform dough forms.
  3. Refrigerate 20 minutes until firm enough to roll.
  4. Roll into 1 Tbsp balls.
  5. Refrigerate or freeze until set.

Notes

  • Espresso powder gives more concentrated coffee flavor than instant coffee; King Arthur and Medaglia d’Oro are widely available
  • Cardamom fades quickly once ground — freshly ground pods make a noticeable difference
  • If dough is too sticky, add 1-2 Tbsp oat flour or more oats; if too dry, add 1 tsp water or extra honey
  • Refrigerate up to 2 weeks or freeze up to 3 months; makes ~16-18 balls
